Tomáš Stúpala (born 5 May 1966 in Bratislava) is a former Slovak football player.


== Career ==
He played club football for ŠK Slovan Bratislava. He earned 14 caps for the Slovak national football team from 1994 to 1995.After he retired from playing, became a football coach. He was appointed assistant manager of ŠK Slovan's B side in 2008.
