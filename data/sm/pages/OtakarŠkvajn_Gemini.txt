Otakar Škvajn (3 June 1894 – 12 September 1941) was a Czechoslovak footballer. He competed in the men's tournament at the 1920 Summer Olympics.On a club level, he played for SK Kladno, SK Židenice, Moravská Slavia Brno, SK Slavia Prague and AC Sparta Prague.He was forced to end his active career at the age of 26 due to the knee injury. Afterwards, Škvajn began to work as a football manager, coaching i.a. SK Prostějov, SK Kladno, Pogoń Lwów and Wisła Kraków.
