Clegg Hoyt (December 10, 1910 â€“ October 6, 1967) was an American film and television actor. He appeared in over 100 films and television programs, and was perhaps best known for his silent role as the Sportscaster's sidekick, George, in the 1963 film Son of Flubber, appearing in a scene with actor, comedian and game show panelist Paul Lynde. Hoyt also played the recurring role of Mac in 13 episodes of the American medical drama television series Dr. Kildare. He died in October 1967 of a stroke in Woodland Hills, California. Hoyt was buried in Babylon Cemetery.
