Vladimir Aleksandrovich Ridel (Russian: Владимир Александрович Ридель; born 22 June 1985) is a Russian former professional football player.


== Club career ==
He made his Russian Football National League debut for FC Zhemchuzhina-Sochi on 27 March 2010 in a game against FC Baltika Kaliningrad.
