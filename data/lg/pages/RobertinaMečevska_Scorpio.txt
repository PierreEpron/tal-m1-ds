Robertina Mečevska (Macedonian: Робертина Мечевска; born 16 November 1984) is a retired Macedonian handball player who played for ŽRK Vardar and for the North Macedonia women's national handball team.She plays on the position line player-pivot.
In the season 2010/11, playing for ŽRK Metalurg, she made it to the Last 16 of the Women's EHF Cup Winners' Cup.
