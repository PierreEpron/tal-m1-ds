Salem Gamal Mohammed Ali Al-Harsh (Arabic: سالم جمال محمد علي الهارش; born October 7 1998) is a Yemeni professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Yemeni club Al-Wehda Aden and the Yemen national team.


== International career ==
Al-Harsh was part of the Yemeni squad that played at the 2019 AFC Asian Cup.
