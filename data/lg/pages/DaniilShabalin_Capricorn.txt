Daniil Vladimirovich Shabalin (Russian: Даниил Владимирович Шабалин; born 3 January 1998) is a Russian professional football player. He plays as a left midfielder or right midfielder for Spartak Kostroma.


== Club career ==
He made his Russian Football National League debut for FC Baltika Kaliningrad on 4 August 2018 in a game against FC Luch Vladivostok.
