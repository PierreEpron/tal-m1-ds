Faouzi Mansouri (Arabic: فوزي منصوري; 17 January 1956 – 18 May 2022) was an Algerian professional footballer who played as a defender. He played mostly in France with Montpellier. For the Algeria national team he participated at two editions of FIFA World Cup, in 1982 and 1986.
