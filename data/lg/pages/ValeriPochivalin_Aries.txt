Valeri Eduardovich Pochivalin (Russian: Валерий Эдуардович Почивалин; born 11 April 1992) is a Russian football defender who plays for Veles Moscow.


== Club career ==
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Zvezda Ryazan on 31 July 2012 in a game against FC Metallurg Lipetsk.
He made his Russian Football National League debut for FC Baltika Kaliningrad on 16 July 2016 in a game against FC Tyumen.
On 12 August 2019, he signed with FC Khimki.On 26 June 2020, he joined Russian Premier League club Rotor Volgograd. On 9 September 2020, his Rotor contract was cancelled by mutual consent before he made any appearances for the club. On 14 September 2020 he signed with Fakel Voronezh.
