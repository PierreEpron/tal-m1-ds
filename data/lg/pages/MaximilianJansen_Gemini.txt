Maximilian Jansen (born 26 May 1993) is a German professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for FSV Zwickau.


== Career ==
On 18 June 2019, Jansen joined R.E. Virton on a two-year contract with an option for one further year. However, he left the club again already at the end of August due to personal reasons.
