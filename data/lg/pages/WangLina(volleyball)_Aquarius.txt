Wang Lina (simplified Chinese: 王丽娜; traditional Chinese: 王麗娜; pinyin: wáng lì nà; born 5 February 1978 in Heilongjiang) is the Former China women's national volleyball team Outside Hitter. She won the 2003 World Cup and 2004 Olympics gold medal.


== Clubs ==
 Bayi (Army)(1995-2008)
 Guangzhou Jianlong (2009–2010)
