Roman Zhuravskyi (Ukrainian: Роман Петрович Журавський; 3 June 1948 – 19 February 2017) was a Ukrainian professional football player who played as defender.


== Playing and coaching career ==
Zhuravskyi made his professional career in the different football teams of the Ukrainian SSR.After his retirement he lived in Briukhovychi.


== Death ==
Zhuravskyi died on 19 February 2017 at the age of 68.


== Honours ==
Soviet Top League (1) with FC Dynamo Kyiv: 1971
Master of Sport of the USSR (1971)
