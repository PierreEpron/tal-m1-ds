Julian Emilio Vitug Ejercito (Tagalog pronunciation: [ɛhɛɹˈsɪtɔ]; born January 15, 1996), more popularly known by his screen name Julian Estrada, is a Filipino DJ and actor. He is a talent of ABS-CBN and Star Magic and part of the Ejercito-Estrada clan. He was born to Senator Jinggoy Estrada, a politician and former actor who has been a member of the Senate of the Philippines since 2004, and to Ma. Presentacion "Precy" Vitug.
