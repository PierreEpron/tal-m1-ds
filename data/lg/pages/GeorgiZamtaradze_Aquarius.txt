Georgi Tamazievich Zamtaradze (Russian: Георгий Тамазиевич Замтарадзе; born ( 1987-02-12)12 February 1987) is a Russian male futsal player, playing as a goalkeeper. He is part of the Russia national futsal team. He competed at the UEFA Futsal Euro 2016. At club level he is playing for Dinamo Moskva  in Russia in 2016.
