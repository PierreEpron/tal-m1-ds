Valery Karnitsky (Belarusian: Валеры Карнiцкi; Russian: Валерий Карницкий; born 20 August 1991) is a Belarusian professional footballer.His elder brother Alyaksandr Karnitsky is also a professional footballer.


== Honours ==
Dinamo Brest

Belarusian Cup winner: 2016–17
