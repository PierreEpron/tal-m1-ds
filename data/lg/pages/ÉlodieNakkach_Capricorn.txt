Nahla Élodie Nakkach (born 20 January 1995), known as Élodie Nakkach (Arabic: ائلودي نقاش), is a professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Swiss Women's Super League club Servette. Born in France, she represents Morocco at international level.


== International career ==
Nakkach was capped for Morocco at senior level during the 2018 Africa Women Cup of Nations qualification (first round).
