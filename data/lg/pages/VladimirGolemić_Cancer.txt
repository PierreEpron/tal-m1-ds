Vladimir Golemić (Serbian Cyrillic: Владимир Големић; born 28 June 1991) is a Serbian professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for Italian Serie C club Crotone.


== Club career ==
On 31 January 2022, Golemić returned to Crotone on a contract until 30 June 2022, with a conditional extension option based on performance.


== Honours ==
Mladost LučaniSerbian First League: 2013–14
