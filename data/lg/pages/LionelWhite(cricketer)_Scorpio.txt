Born at Wateringbury, Kent, White was a right-handed batsman.White made his debut in first-class cricket for Kent County Cricket Club in 1869 against Sussex, which he followed up with three further first-class appearances, playing twice against Nottinghamshire and once against Surrey. White scored 84 runs in his four matches, with a highest score of 34.He died at Tunbridge Wells in June 1917. His cousin Edward White was also a first-class cricketer.
