Richard George Petrie (born 23 August 1967) is a former New Zealand international cricketer who played 12 One Day Internationals.In February 2020, he was named in New Zealand's squad for the Over-50s Cricket World Cup in South Africa. However, the tournament was cancelled during the third round of matches due to the coronavirus pandemic.
