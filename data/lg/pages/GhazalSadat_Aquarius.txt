Ghazal Sadat (Persian: غزال سادات) is an Afghan singer and businesswoman.


== Life ==
Ghazal Sadat was born in Herat.Sadat is currently trading in Dubai. She has invested in a water company, Mediacal Clinics and also owns four restaurants and cafes in the Jumeirah area in Dubai, and Riyadh and Jeddah in Saudi Arabia   .
