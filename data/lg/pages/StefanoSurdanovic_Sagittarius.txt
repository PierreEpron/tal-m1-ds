Stefano Surdanovic (Serbian Cyrillic: Стефано Сурдановић, romanized: Stefano Surdanović, born 23 November 1998) is an Austrian professional footballer who plays for Austria Lustenau.


== Club career ==
He made his Austrian Football First League debut for SV Ried on 21 July 2017 in a game against SC Wiener Neustadt.On 3 February 2022, Surdanovic signed with Admira Wacker.


== Personal life ==
Born in Austria, Surdanovic is of Serbian descent and holds a Serbian passport.
