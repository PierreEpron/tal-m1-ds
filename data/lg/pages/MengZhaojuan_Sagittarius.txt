Meng Zhaojuan (Chinese: 孟昭娟; born 14 December 1989) is a road and track cyclist, who most recently rode for UCI Women's Continental Team China Liv Pro Cycling. Representing Hong Kong, Meng competed at the 2009, 2010, 2011 and 2015 UCI Track Cycling World Championships. Meng was born in Tongliao, Inner Mongolia, China, and moved to Hong Kong in 2006.
