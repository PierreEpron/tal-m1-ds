Giedrė Rakauskaitė  (born 13 June 1991 in Kaunas) is a British Paralympic rower who is a triple World champion in the mixed coxed four.She won a gold medal in the mixed cox four at the 2020 Tokyo Paralympics.Rakauskaitė was appointed Member of the Order of the British Empire (MBE) in the 2022 New Year Honours for services to rowing.
