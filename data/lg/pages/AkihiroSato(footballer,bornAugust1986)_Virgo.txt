Akihiro Sato (佐藤 昭大, Satō Akihiro, born 30 August 1986 in Asahi, Mie) is a Japanese footballer who plays for Montedio Yamagata in the J2 League.


== Club career ==
After four season with Kashima Antlers, he was released by the club in November 2015.
