Emad Bahavar (Persian: عماد بهاور) is an Iranian political activist affiliated with the Freedom Movement of Iran and currently holding office as head of the party's youth wing.Bahavar was arrested following the post-election protests in 2009, and released after five years of imprisonment. He went on hunger strike in 2011, along with eleven other political prisoners.Bahavar registered to run for a City Council of Tehran seat in 2017 elections, however he was disqualified by the authorities.
