Sajjad Mashayekhi (Persian: سجاد مشایخی, born February 23, 1994) is an Iranian professional basketball player and gold medal at Asian Cup 2012 Tokyo and 2014 Wuhan and 2016 Tehran He has played for the Iranian national basketball team.
He attended the 2014 World Cup in Spain with the Iranian national basketball team and played in the 2019 World Cup in China with the Iranian national basketball team.
