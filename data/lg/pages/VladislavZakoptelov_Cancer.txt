Vladislav Mikhailovich Zakoptelov (Russian: Владислав Михайлович Закоптелов; born 26 June 1988) is a former Russian football striker.


== Club career ==
He made his debut for the senior squad of FC Amkar Perm on 20 September 2006 in the Russian Cup game against FC Angusht Nazran.
