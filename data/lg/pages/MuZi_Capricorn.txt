Mu Zi (Chinese: 木子; pinyin: Mù Zǐ; born 9 January 1989) is a Chinese female table tennis player. She is a two-time finalist in mixed doubles event at the World Championships. In 2015, she reached the semifinal of her first women's singles event at the World Championships and in 2019 she won with Chen Ke the Hong Kong Open Women's Doubles.
