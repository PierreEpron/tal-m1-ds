Patrícia Kovács (born 26 May 1996) is an Austrian handballer for Mosonmagyaróvári KC SE and the Austria national team.


== International honours ==
WHA:
Winner: 2016, 2017


== Personal life ==
She is the daughter of Hypo Niederösterreich's coach, Ferenc Kovács, who relocated to Austria. Her sister, Klaudia Kovács, is a handball player as well.
