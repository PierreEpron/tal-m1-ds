Gerald Tusha (born 5 February 1991 in Tiranë) is an Albanian professional footballer who plays as a left midfielder for FK Vora.


== Club career ==
Tusha made his league debut for Tirana on the opening day of the 2008–2009 season on 24 August 2008 during a match with Vllaznia Shkodër. The 17-year-old midfielder came on for Lek Kcira in the 55th minute of the game. During the 2008–09 season he made 2 league appearances, both of them coming on as a substitute. In total he played 54 minutes of Albanian Superliga football.In August 2016, Tusha joined Xagħra United of Malta.After two years in Malta, Tusha signed a one-year contract with Liria Prizren in Kosovo.
