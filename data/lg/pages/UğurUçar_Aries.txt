Uğur Uçar (born 5 April 1987) is a Turkish professional footballer who plays as a right back.


== Career ==
Uçar is a product of the Galatasaray youth system. He made his first team debut on 7 March 2004 against Adanaspor. He spent the 2006–07 season on loan at Kayserispor, playing in 22 matches. Upon his return to Galatasaray in 2007, Uçar became the first choice right back, playing in 26 matches before injuring his right knee in a match against Konyaspor on 18 February 2008. The injury kept him from competitive play for 15 months, making his return on 30 May 2009 against Sivasspor. Uçar played in 20 matches the following season.He was transferred to Ankaragücü for a fee of €1.3 million on 23 June 2010.
