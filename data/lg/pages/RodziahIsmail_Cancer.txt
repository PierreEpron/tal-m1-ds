Rodziah binti Ismail (born 8 July 1964) is a Malaysian politician who has served as Member of the Selangor State Executive Council (EXCO) in the Pakatan Rakyat (PR) and Pakatan Harapan (PH) state administrations under Menteris Besar Khalid Ibrahim, Azmin Ali and Amirudin Shari from March 2008 to her resignation in August 2014 and again since May 2018, Member of Parliament (MP) for Ampang since November 2022 and Member of the Selangor State Legislative Assembly (MLA) for Batu Tiga since March 2008. She is a member of the People's Justice Party (PKR), a component party of PH and formerly PR coalitions.
