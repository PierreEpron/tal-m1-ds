Giannis Papakostas (Greek: Γιάννης Παπακώστας) is a Greek football manager. Papakostas began his coaching career at Eleusina and Chalkidona and PAS Giannina that he brought into the semi-final of Greek Cup in the 2006–07 season. Papakostas later managed Panserraikos and Kavala FC.
