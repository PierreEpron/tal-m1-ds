Yuri Vladimirovich Kuleshov (Russian: Юрий Владимирович Кулешов; born 12 April 1981) is a Russian professional football coach and a former defensive midfielder. He is an assistant coach with FC Ryazan.


== Career ==
In March 2015, Kuleshov signed for FC Sakhalin Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk.
