Aarav Chowdhary (born 23 November 1981) is an Indian actor and model. He is best known for his role as Bhishma in the TV show Mahabharat. He is from Jaipur. He is an Indian actor  who appeared in several Bollywood movies such as Dhoom and Housefull 3. He got fame for his role as Bhishma Pitamah in the 2013 edition of Mahabharat on television. He has also acted in a television series named Iss Pyaar Ko Kya Naam Doon? Ek Baar Phir.
