Intharat Apinyakool (Thai: อินทรัตน์ อภิญญากุล, born 30 May 1982) is a Thai retired professional footballer who played as a goalkeeper.


== International career ==
He played for Thailand at the 1999 FIFA U-17 World Championship in New Zealand and the 2005 FIFA Beach Soccer World Cup in Brazil.
