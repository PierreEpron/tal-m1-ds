Todor Krastev (Bulgarian: Тодор Кръстев; 1 June 1945 – 9 May 2000) was a Bulgarian footballer. He was born in Stara Zagora. He competed at the 1968 Summer Olympics in Mexico City, where he won a silver medal with the Bulgarian team.
