Jonatan Straus (born 30 June 1994) is a Polish professional footballer who plays as a left-back for II liga club Radunia Stężyca.


== Career ==
Straus left Jagiellonia Białystok at the end of the 2018–19 season. At the end of July 2019, he then joined KS Legionovia Legionowo. Six months later, Straus moved to I liga club Stomil Olsztyn.On 20 June 2022, he left Stomil by mutual consent to join II liga side Radunia Stężyca.
