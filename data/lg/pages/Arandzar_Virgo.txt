Missak Kouyumjian (Armenian: Միսաք Գույումճյան; September 8, 1877 Caesarea, Ankara Vilayet, Ottoman Empire - September 13, 1913 Adana, Ottoman Turkey), better known by his pen name Arandzar (Armenian: Առանձար), was an Armenian short story writer, poet, and principal.
