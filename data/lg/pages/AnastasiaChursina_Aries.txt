Anastasia Aleksandrovna Chursina (Анастасия Александровна Чурсина; née Yakovenko; born 7 April 1995) is a Russian racing cyclist, who most recently rode for UCI Women's WorldTeam Alé BTC Ljubljana. She rode at the 2014 UCI Road World Championships.
