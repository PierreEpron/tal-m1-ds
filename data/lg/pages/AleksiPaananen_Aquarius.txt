Aleksi Paananen (born 25 January 1993) is a Finnish professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Veikkausliiga club HJK. He is often labeled as Thiago Alcantara or Diego Maradona of Finnish football, of which the latter he got the nickname ”Diego”.


== Career ==


=== FC Inter Turku ===
On 1 November 2018, Inter Turku announced the signing of Paananen for the 2019 season. He signed a 2-year deal.


=== HJK ===
On 2 November 2022, Paananen signed a contract with HJK for the 2023 season, with an option for 2024.
