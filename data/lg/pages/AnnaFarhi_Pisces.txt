Anna Farhi (born (1980-03-01)1 March 1980) is an Israeli female former volleyball player, born in Bulgaria, who plays as a middle blocker. She was part of the Israel women's national volleyball team from 2011 to 2015. She competed at the 2011 and 2015 Women's European Volleyball Championships.


== History ==
In her youth, Farhi played for Bulgaria's under-17 team before travelling to Switzerland at the age of 21. While playing an exhibition game in Switzerland, the coach of Israel's women's national volleyball team convinced her to come to Israel and play for their national team.
