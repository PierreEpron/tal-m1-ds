Vladimir Anatolyevich Ivanov (Russian: Владимир Анатольевич Иванов) is a Russian professional Grand Prix motorcycle racer. In 2010 and 2011 he raced under a Ukrainian racing license. In 2013 he raced in the Supersport World Championship for Kawasaki DMC-Lorenzini Team.
