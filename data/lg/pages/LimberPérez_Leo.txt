Limber Omar Pérez Mauricio (born July 26, 1976), his first name also spelled Limbert or Limberth, is a retired Honduran football defender.


== Club career ==
He is one of few players who have played for Honduras' Big Four: Olimpia, Marathón, Real España and F.C. Motagua.


== International career ==
Pérez made his debut for Honduras in a July 2001 friendly match against Ecuador and has earned a total of 9 caps, scoring no goals. He has represented his country in 1 FIFA World Cup qualification match and was a member of the national squad that beat Brazil in Copa América 2001 in Colombia.His final international was a March 2002 friendly match against the United States.
