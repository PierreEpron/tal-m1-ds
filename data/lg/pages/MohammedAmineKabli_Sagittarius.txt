Mohammed Kabli is a Moroccan footballer. He usually plays midfielder and is a member of FAR Rabat.
Kabli has played for Moghreb Tétouan, whom he joined from AS Salé in August 2006. He won the 2008 Coupe du Trône with FAR Rabat.He has been called into camp for the Morocco national football team.
