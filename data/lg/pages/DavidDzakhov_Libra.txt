David Yuryevich Dzakhov (Russian: Давид Юрьевич Дзахов; born 6 October 1988) is a Russian professional footballer. He plays for FC Alania Vladikavkaz.


== Club career ==
He made his debut in the Russian Premier League on 26 April 2009 for FC Amkar Perm.After his contract with FC Amkar Perm expired in summer of 2015, he took half a year away from professional football, before rejoining Amkar in January 2016.
