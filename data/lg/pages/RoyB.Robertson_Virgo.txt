Roy B. Robertson (September 9, 1919 – March 1, 2000) was an American football coach and college athletics administrator. He served as the head football coach at Nebraska Wesleyan University in Lincoln, Nebraska from 1950 to 1953 and Colorado College in Colorado Springs, Colorado from 1954 to 1956, compiling a career college football coaching record of 20–44–4.Robertson letters in football, basketball, track at from McPherson College in McPherson, Kansas before graduating in 1940.
