Masato Furuno (古野 正人, Furuno Masato, born September 27, 1986 in Takarazuka, Hyōgo Prefecture, Japan) is a  former professional Japanese baseball player. He played pitcher for the Tokyo Yakult Swallows. He has been a batting practice pitcher for the Hanshin Tigers since 2018.
