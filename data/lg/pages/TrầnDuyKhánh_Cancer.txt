Trần Duy Khánh (born 20 July 1997) is a Vietnamese footballer who plays as a midfielder for Becamex Bình Dương.


== Honours ==
Becamex Bình Dương

Vietnamese National Cup: 2018; Runner-up 2017
Vietnamese Super Cup: 2016; Runner-up 2019
