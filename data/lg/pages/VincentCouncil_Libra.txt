== High school career ==
Council played high school basketball at The Patterson School, in Lenoir, North Carolina.


== College career ==
Council played college basketball with the Providence Friars, from 2009 to 2013.


== Professional career ==
Council began his pro career in with  Hapoel Tel Aviv in 2013. In June 2014, he signed with Rethymno of the Greek Basket League. On September 22, 2015, Council joined Koroivos Amaliadas.On June 9, 2017, Council joined BC Nokia of the Korisliiga.
