Margita Alfvén (November 16, 1905 – March 11, 1962) was a Danish-born Swedish film actress of the silent and early sound era.She was the daughter of the composer Hugo Alfvén and the artist Marie Krøyer.


== Selected filmography ==
Her Little Majesty (1925)
The Million Dollars (1926)
Uncle Frans (1926)
Jansson's Temptation (1928)
Parisiennes (1928)
The Way Through the Night (1929)
Three from the Unemployment Office (1932)
Girls to Marry (1932)
