Christopher George Knight (born 1 December 1972) is a former English cricketer. Knight was a right-handed batsman who bowled right-arm medium pace. He was born at Taunton in 1972.Knight made his List-A debut for the Hampshire Cricket Board in the 1st Round of the 2001 Cheltenham and Gloucester Trophy against the Kent Cricket Board. Knight's final List-A match came in 2nd Round of the 2002 Cheltenham and Gloucester Trophy, which was played in 2001 against Ireland.
Knight made a single appearance in the 1993 Second Eleven Championship for Hampshire Second XI against Leicestershire Second XI.
