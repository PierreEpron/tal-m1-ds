Gary Ambroise (born 17 July 1985) is a professional footballer who plays for Belgian club White Star Bruxelles, as a striker.
Born in France, he represents Haiti at international level.


== Career ==
Ambroise has played in France for FC Les Lilas and US Roye-Noyon, and in Belgium for Tubize.He made his international debut for Haiti in 2011, and has appeared in FIFA World Cup qualifying matches.
