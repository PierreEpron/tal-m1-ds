Bohdan Lytvyak (Ukrainian: Богдан Олегович Литвяк; born 5 May 1998) is a Ukrainian football midfielder.


== Career ==
Lytvyak is a product of his native Kryvbas Kryvyi Rih youth sportive system.He made his debut in the Ukrainian Premier League for Inhulets Petrove as a second half-time substituted player in a home drawing match against FC Mariupol on 25 October 2020.
