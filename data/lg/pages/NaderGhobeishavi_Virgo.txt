Nader Ghobeishavi (Persian: نادر غبیشاوی; born September 19, 1975), is an Iranian football coach and a former player, who played as a goalkeeper. He currently works as a goalkeeping coach with Foolad in the Iran Pro League.


== Club career ==
Ghobeishavi Previously played for Aboomoslem and Rah Ahan, both of whom participate in Iran Pro League.


== International ==
In 2003, Ghobeishavi was summoned by Iranian Football Federation to attend a national camp in preparation for the Asian Nations Cup.
