Charles Edward Stuart (born November 12, 1935) is a Canadian former professional hockey center.


== Career ==
Stuart played in the Eastern Hockey League for the Johnstown Jets, Charlotte Clippers, Philadelphia Ramblers, Knoxville Knights and Charlotte Checkers.
