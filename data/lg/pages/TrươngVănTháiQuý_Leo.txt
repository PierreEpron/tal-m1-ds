Trương Văn Thái Quý (born 22 August 1997) is a Vietnamese professional footballer who plays as a defensive midfielder or a right back for V.League 1 club Hà Nội and the Vietnam national team.


== Honours ==


=== Club ===
Hà Nội

Vietnamese National Cup: 2019, 2020, 2022

Southeast Asian Games: 2019
