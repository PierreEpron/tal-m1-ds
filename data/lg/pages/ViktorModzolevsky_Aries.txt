Viktor Modzolevsky (Russian: Виктор Игоревич Модзолевский; 13 April 1943 – 20 November 2011) was a Soviet fencer. He won a silver medal in the team épée event at the 1968 Summer Olympics and a bronze in the same event at the 1972 Summer Olympics. He was a coach of the Russian national team in the 1990s.
Modzolevsky died in a road accident on 20 November 2011 in way Tula - Voronezh (Don Highway).  He was 68.
