David Wijns (born 9 January 1987) is a Belgian footballer who currently plays for Heist in the Belgian First Amateur Division.


== Career ==
Wijns started his professional football career with Heist in the Belgian Promotion, helping the team promote twice to end up in the Belgian Second Division before being transferred to Belgian Pro League team Kortrijk in 2011. For Kortrijk, he played 29 league games in two seasons, before moving back to his birth town to join OH Leuven in 2013.
