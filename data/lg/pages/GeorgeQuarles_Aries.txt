George Quarles (born March 24, 1967) is an American football coach who is currently the head coach at East Tennessee State University, a role he has held since December of 2021. Prior to East Tennessee State, he was the associate head coach, offensive coordinator, and quarterbacks coach at Furman University, where he played college football.
