Uğur Demirok (born 8 July 1988 in Eyüp, Istanbul, Turkey) is a Turkish footballer who plays as a defender for Süper Lig club Kayserispor.Demirok earned his first cap for Turkey in a friendly match against Sweden on 5 March 2014.
