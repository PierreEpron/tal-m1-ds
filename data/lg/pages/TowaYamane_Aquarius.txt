Towa Yamane (山根 永遠, Yamane Towa, born February 5, 1999) is a Japanese football player. He plays for Zweigen Kanazawa on loan from Cerezo Osaka. His father is Iwao Yamane.


== Career ==
Towa Yamane joined J1 League club Cerezo Osaka in 2017. On 27 June 2019, Yamane was loaned out to Zweigen Kanazawa.
