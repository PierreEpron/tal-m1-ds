Mohamad Jaamour (Arabic: محمد جعمور) (born 15 October 1991 in Al Houlah, Homs, Syria) is a Syrian footballer. He currently plays for Al-Karamah, which competes in the Syrian Premier League the top division in Syria. He plays as a midfielder, wearing the number 42 jersey for Al-Karamah.


== Club career ==
Jaamour signed for Al-Karamah as a youth team player on 2010 and rose through the club's youth sides. On 25 January 2011 he made his first competitive appearance for Al-Karamah in a 3–1 victory against Al-Majd in the Syrian Premier League.
