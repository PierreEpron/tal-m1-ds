Eros Danilo Araújo (born November 6, 1956 in Rio Bom, Paraná) is a Brazilian politician, former mayor of Telêmaco Borba, municipality in the state of Paraná in the Southern Region of Brazil.His term as mayor ended in 2012. He is a physician trained at the UFPR - Federal University of Paraná. He is from the Brazilian Democratic Movement Party (PMDB).
