Scott Robert Doffek (born July 31, 1968) is an American baseball coach and former infielder, who is the current head baseball coach of the Milwaukee Panthers. He attended college at Waukesha County Technical College, where he played on the school baseball team before embarking on a five-year minor league baseball career in the Los Angeles Dodgers organization.
