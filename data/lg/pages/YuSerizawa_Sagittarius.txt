Yu Serizawa (芹澤 優, Serizawa Yū, born December 3, 1994) is a Japanese voice actress and singer associated with 81 Produce. She is a member of the idol voice actor group i☆Ris. In 2019, she won the Best Supporting Actress Award with Nao Tōyama in the 13th Seiyu Awards.


== Personal life ==
Serizawa is the niece of singer Yuki Saito.
