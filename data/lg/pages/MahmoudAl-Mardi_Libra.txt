Mahmoud Nayef Ahmad Al-Mardi (Arabic: مَحمُود نَايِف أَحمَد الْمَرضِيّ) is a Jordanian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Kuwait Premier League club Qadsia and the Jordan national team.


== Club career ==


=== Kedah Darul Aman ===
On 19 June 2022, Mahmoud Al Mardi signed for Malaysia Super League club Kedah Darul Aman. He scored on his debut against Kaya FC and he scored hattrick against Visakha FC in AFC Cup knockout stage.
