He served as Mayor of Lucca, Tuscany from July 1994 to June 1998, leading a centre-left government formed by the Democratic Party of the Left, the Italian Republican Party and the Social Christians. He was the first leftist mayor in the history of Lucca and the first directly elected by the citizens.Lazzarini died in Lucca on his 93rd birthday in 2020.
