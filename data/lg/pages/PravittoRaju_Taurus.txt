Pravitto Raju (born 25 April 1997) is an Indian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Mumbai Kenkre in the I-League.


== Career ==
Pravitto kicked off his football career, playing for RBI in the first division during his college days. He played an instrumental role in earning RBI their promotion to the senior division football league. At the end of the season, it was Indian Bank Recreational Club assistant coach Noel, who spotted his talent and signed him on at the club.
