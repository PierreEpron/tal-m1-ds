Alessio Donnarumma (born 16 February 1998) is an Italian football player. He plays for Benevento.


== Club career ==
He started his youth career with Benevento, before switching to Inter in 2012. In 2016 he returned to Benevento.He made sporadic bench appearances for Benevento's senior squad in 2016–17 Serie B and 2017–18 Serie A, but did not see any field time.
On 9 July 2018 he joined Serie C club Vibonese on a season-long loan. He made his Serie C debut for Vibonese on 16 September 2018 in a game against Bisceglie as a starter and was substituted at half-time.On 15 July 2019, he joined Picerno in Serie C on loan.


== International ==
In 2014, during his time with Inter, he was called up for several friendlies to the Italy national under-16 football team.
