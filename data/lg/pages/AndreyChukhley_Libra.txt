Andrey Chukhley (Belarusian: Андрэй Чухлей; Russian: Андрей Чухлей; born 2 October 1987) is a Belarusian football midfielder who plays for Ostrovets.


== Career ==
Chukhley left FC Tyumen in December 2014.On 11 August 2016, Andrey Chukhley joined A Lyga club Kauno Žalgiris. He moved to another A Lyga team Jonava in 2016–17 winter transfer window, but decided to leave the club after 4 months.On 19 March 2019, Chukhley left Ararat Yerevan by mutual consent.
