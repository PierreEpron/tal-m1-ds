Pedro Bispo Moreira Júnior (born January 29, 1987, in Santana do Araguaia, Brazil), or simply Pedro Júnior, is a Brazilian striker.


== Club career ==
On 13 January 2020, it was confirmed that Júnior would join the Thai League club Samut Prakan City for the 2020 season.
