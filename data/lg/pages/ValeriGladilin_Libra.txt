Valeri Pavlovich Gladilin (Russian: Валерий Павлович Гладилин; born 19 October 1951) is a Russian politician and a former professional football coach and a player. He works as a president of FC Dnepr Smolensk. He also serves in the State Duma (elected from the United Russia party). From 2004 to 2008 he served in the Federation Council of Russia.


== Honours ==
Soviet Top League runner-up: 1974, 1983, 1984.
