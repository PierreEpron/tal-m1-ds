Andrea Pierobon  (born 19 July 1969) is an Italian football coach and a former player who played mostly for Cittadella as a goalkeeper. He works as a goalkeeping coach with Cittadella. He holds the record for being the oldest professional player in the history of Italian football.Pierobon rejoined the team that originally signed him to act as a role model for the youth on the squad.
