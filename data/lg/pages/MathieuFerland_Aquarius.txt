Mathieu Ferland (born January 26, 1974) is a Canadian video game producer best known for producing the Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell series for Ubisoft Montreal. IGN has named him the 75th greatest game creator of all time [1].


== Notable games ==
Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell (2002)
Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell: Pandora Tomorrow (2004)
Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell: Chaos Theory (2005)
Assassin's Creed (2007)
Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell: Double Agent (2008)
Tom Clancy's Splinter Cell: Conviction 2010
