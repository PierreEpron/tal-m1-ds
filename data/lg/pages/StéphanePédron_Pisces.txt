Stéphane Pedron (born 20 February 1971) is a French former professional footballer who played as a midfielder. 


== Career ==
Pedron was voted 1997–98 French Division 2 Player of the Season after helping Lorient to promotion. Pedron retired from football following the expiry of his contract with Lorient after the 2006–07 season. Afterwards, he joined the technical staff of the club alongside Christophe Le Roux.
