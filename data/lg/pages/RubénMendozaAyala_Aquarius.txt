Rubén Mendoza Ayala (2 February 1961 – 16 April 2016) was a Mexican politician who was affiliated with the Party of the Democratic Revolution. He served as Deputy of the LVII and LIX Legislatures of the Mexican Congress representing the State of Mexico.He later was Municipal President of Tlalnepantla from 2000 to 2003. In 2005 he ran against Enrique Peña Nieto to the Governature of the State. In 2009 he was disaffiliated of the National Action Party due to irregular financial managements of the party's accounts. In 2012 he affiliated with the Party of the Democratic Revolution, where he ran again to the Governature.
