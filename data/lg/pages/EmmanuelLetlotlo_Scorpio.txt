Emmanuel Letlotlo (born 15 November 1995) is a South African soccer player who plays as a forward for ABC Motsepe League side Postmasburg FC.


== Career ==
Born in Soweto, Letlotlo signed a three-year contract with ABSA Premiership side Kaizer Chiefs on 31 May 2016 after becoming the top scorer in the reserve league. He made his professional debut for the club on 23 August 2016 in the league against Bidvest Wits.
