Ambrosiy Anzorovych Chachua (Ukrainian: Амбросій Анзорович Чачуа; born 2 April 1994) is a Ukrainian professional football defender who plays for Karpaty Lviv.


== Career ==
Chachua is a product of FC Veres Rivne and of the UFK Lviv School Systems. His first trainer was Yaroslav Dmytrasevych. He made his debut for FC Karpaty entering in the second time in the match against FC Chornomorets Odesa on 3 November 2013 in the Ukrainian Premier League.He also played for the Ukrainian under-18 national football team and was called up for other age level representations.
