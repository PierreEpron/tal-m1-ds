Vladlen Igorevich Babayev (Russian: Владлен Игоревич Бабаев; born 16 October 1996) is a Russian football player. He plays for FC KamAZ Naberezhnye Chelny.


== Club career ==
He made his debut in the Russian Football National League for FC Volgar Astrakhan on 15 July 2017 in a game against FC Rotor Volgograd.
