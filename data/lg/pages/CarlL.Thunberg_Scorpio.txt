Carl L. Thunberg, FSAScot (born 25 October 1963) is a Swedish historian, archaeologist and popular historian.


== Biography ==
Thunberg was educated at the University of Gothenburg and Uppsala University. From 2010 to 2012 he had a leading part in the policy struggle to preserve the archaeological reconstructions at Gene fornby near Örnsköldsvik in Sweden. He has lectured on didactic method for history as a school subject. Thunberg became a fellow of the Society of Antiquaries of Scotland in 2016. Works by Thunberg that have been especially used by scholars are his studies on Viking Age cultural encounters with the Muslim world and on the Battle of Fýrisvellir. In 2022 he was appointed chairman of Västernorrland County Museum.
