Akihiro Fukatsu (深津 旭弘, Fukatsu Akihiro, born (1987-07-23)23 July 1987) is a Japanese male volleyball player. He was part of the Japan men's national volleyball team. On club level, he plays for Osaka Blazers Sakai.
