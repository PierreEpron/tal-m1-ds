Billie Sørum (née Skjerpen; born 13 August 1948 in Sandvika) is a former Norwegian curler.At the international level, she is a European silver (1991) and bronze (1992) medallist.
At the national level, she is a three-time Norwegian women's champion curler (1986, 1987, 1988).
