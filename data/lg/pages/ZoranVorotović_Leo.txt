Zoran Vorotović (Serbian Cyrillic: Зоран Воротовић, born 12 August 1958) is a former Montenegrin football player who played left-back for clubs in the former Yugoslavia and Turkey.


== Club career ==
Born in Herceg Novi, SR Montenegro, SFR Yugoslavia, Vorotović began playing football for local side FK Sutjeska Nikšić.  Later, he would play in the Yugoslav First League for FK Budućnost Titograd and FK Spartak Subotica.In 1987, he moved to Turkey where he would make 68 Süper Lig appearances in a three-year spell with Sarıyer G.K.


== Personal life ==
He was seriously injured in a traffic accident in 2001.
