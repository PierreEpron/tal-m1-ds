Bastian Reinhardt (born 19 November 1975 in Ludwigslust) is a former German footballer. He is the currently managing the Under-17 squad of Hamburger SV.


== Career ==
Reinhardt began his career 1983 with Empor Grabow/Grabower FC and joined 1. FC Magdeburg in 1992. He left Magdeburg after two years and moved to Hamburg where he joined VfL Hamburg 93. Reinhardt played three years for VfL Hamburg 93 and moved in the summer of 1997 to Hannover 96. In his three years with Hannover 96 he played 58 matches and scored four goals. From 2000 to 2003 Reinhardt played for Arminia Bielefeld 99 matches, scoring eight goals.
