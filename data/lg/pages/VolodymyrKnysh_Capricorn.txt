Volodymyr Knysh (Ukrainian: Володимир Миколайович Книш; born 24 December 1970 Dnipropetrovsk, Ukrainian SSR) is a former Soviet and Ukrainian footballer and Ukrainian football coach who managed FC Naftovyk-Ukrnafta Okhtyrka.
