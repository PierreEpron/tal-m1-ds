Aleksandr Yuryevich Makarov (Russian: Александр Юрьевич Макаров; born 24 April 1996) is a Russian football coach and a former player who played as a right winger. He works at the Chertanovo Education Center.


== Club career ==
He made his debut in the Russian Football National League for FC Baltika Kaliningrad on 27 March 2016 in a game against FC Torpedo Armavir.He made his PFC CSKA Moscow debut on 25 July 2017 in a 2017–18 UEFA Champions League third qualifying round game against AEK Athens.On 9 February 2018, Makarov re-joined FC Tosno on loan until the end of the 2017–18 season.In June 2018, Makarov suffered a torn ACL in his left knee.On 19 February 2019, Makarov joined Avangard Kursk on loan for the remainder of the season.On 30 August 2019, he moved to Avangard on a permanent basis.


=== International ===
He won the 2013 UEFA European Under-17 Championship with Russia national under-17 football team, he also participated with it in the 2013 FIFA U-17 World Cup.
