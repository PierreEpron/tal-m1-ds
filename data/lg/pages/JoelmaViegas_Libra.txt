Joelma Patrícia da Cunha Viegas a.k.a. Cajó, (born October 15, 1986) is a team handball player from Angola. She plays on the Angola women's national handball team, and participated at the 2011 World Women's Handball Championship in Brazil and the 2012 Summer Olympics.At the club level, she plays for Angolan side Primeiro de Agosto at the Angolan Handball league
