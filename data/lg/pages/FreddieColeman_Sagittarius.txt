Freddie Robert John Coleman (born 15 December 1991 in Edinburgh) is a Scottish cricketer who played for Warwickshire County Cricket Club and Scotland.Coleman signed for Warwickshire in March 2013 after graduating from the county's Academy.  He was released in June 2016 at the end of his contract.Coleman made his One Day International debut for Scotland against Kenya on 30 June 2013.
