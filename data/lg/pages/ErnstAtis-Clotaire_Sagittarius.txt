Ernst Atis-Clotaire (born 9 December 1977) is a former Haitian-French footballer, who played three seasons for AS Monaco in Ligue 1.Atis-Clotaire won the 1996 UEFA European Under-18 Championship with France and appeared for the Haiti national football team eleven times during Caribbean Cup competitions in 2000 and 2002.
