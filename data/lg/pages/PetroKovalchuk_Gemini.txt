Petro Kovalchuk (Ukrainian: Петро Ярославович Ковальчук; born 28 May 1984) is a Ukrainian former professional football defender.


== Career ==


=== Club ===
In July 2007, Kovalchuk joined Kryvbas from Spartak Ivano-Frankivsk. Kovalchuk also played then for Dniester Ovidiopol and FC Lviv.
On 1 February 2011, Kovalchuk signed a 1.5-year deal with Chornomorets.In March 2015, Kovalchuk moved to Tajikistan, signing for Tajikistan Higher League Champions FC Istiklol, leaving the club after his contract expired in January 2017.In January 2017 he signed a contract with Maldivian football Club Green Streets, along with another two Ukrainian footballers. He made his debut for CGS in a match against Victory Sports Club on 25 February 2017.
