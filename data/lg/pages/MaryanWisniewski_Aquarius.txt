== Life and career ==
Marian Wisniewski was born on 1 February 1937 in Calonne-Ricouart, Pas-de-Calais. He earned 33 caps and scored 12 goals for the France national team, and played in the 1958 FIFA World Cup when France finished third. He made his international debut on 3 April 1955, thus becoming the youngest footballer to play for France at the age of 18 years and 2 months.Wisniewski died on 3 March 2022, at the age of 85, in Carpentras, Vaucluse. His grandnephew Jonathan Wisniewski is a former professional rugby union player.
