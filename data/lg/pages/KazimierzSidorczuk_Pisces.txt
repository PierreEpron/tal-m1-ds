Kazimierz Sidorczuk (born March 4, 1967 in Debno) is a former Polish footballer (goalkeeper) playing most of the career in Lech Poznań. He also played for Sturm Graz. He finished his career in Kapfenberger SV, with whom he once scored a goal.He played for Poland 14 times.


== Achievements ==
three times championship of Poland with Lech Poznań
once winner Polish Supercup with Lech Poznań
two times champion of Austria with Sturm Graz
two times winner of Austrian Cup with Sturm Graz
