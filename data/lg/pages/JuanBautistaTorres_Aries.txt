Juan Bautista Torres (born 2 April 2002) is an Argentine tennis player.
Torres has a career high ATP singles ranking of 288 achieved on 17 June 2022. He also has a career high doubles ranking of 439 achieved on 25 October 2021.Torres has won 1 ATP Challenger doubles title at the 2021 Challenger de Buenos Aires with Luciano Darderi.
