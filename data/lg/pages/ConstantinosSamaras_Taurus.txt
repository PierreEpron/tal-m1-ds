Constantinos Samaras (Greek: Κωνσταντίνος Σαμάρας; born 18 May 1984 in Nicosia) is a Cypriot footballer who plays for Olympiakos Nicosia as a defender and was the captain of the latter in the 2018-2019 season.He  is a product of the Anorthosis youth academy and he has played there for all of his career except for 6 months (July 2007-January 2008) when he left the club. He rejoined them on the January transfer period
With Anorthosis he has won 2 championships and 1 cup.


== Honours ==


=== Club ===

Cypriot Cup (1): 2007
