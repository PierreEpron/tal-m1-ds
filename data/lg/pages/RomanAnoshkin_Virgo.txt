Roman Sergeyevich Anoshkin (Russian: Роман Сергеевич Аношкин; born 31 August 1987) is a Russian sprint canoeist. He won individual bronze medals at the 2015 World Championships and 2016 Rio Olympics. He also won a silver medal in the four-man K-4 1000 m event at the 2016 European Championships and placed ninth at the 2016 Olympics. Following this, he competed in the 2020 Olympics.
