Luis Alberto Nuñez Charales (born 10 December 1983 in Santa Marta) is a Colombian former footballer who played as a left back.
His form in the domestic league has led him to his first call up to the Colombia national football team for the FIFA World Cup qualifier match against VenezuelaOn 15 March 2011, he scored a goal in the second half against the Mexican club San Luis in the Copa Libertadores.
