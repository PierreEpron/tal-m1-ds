Carlo De Risio (Vasto 3 June 1991) is an Italian football player. He plays for Serie C Group C club Monopoli.


== Club career ==
He made his Serie C debut for Benevento on 29 August 2010 in a game against Virtus Lanciano.On 19 August 2018, he signed a two-year contract with Catanzaro with an option for third year.On 15 September 2020 he moved to Bari. On 8 January 2022, he was loaned to Pescara.On 1 September 2022, De Risio signed a two-year contract with Monopoli.
