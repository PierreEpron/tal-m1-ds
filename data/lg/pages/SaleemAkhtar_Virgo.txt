Raja Saleem Akhtar (Punjabi, Urdu: راجہ سلیم اختر)                                                                                                   (8 September 1930 – 22 April 2004) was a Pakistani cricketer who played for Multan and Sargodha. His sons Wasim Raja and Rameez Raja played for the Pakistan cricket team, while Zaeem Raja played first-class cricket.
He worked as a civil servant.
