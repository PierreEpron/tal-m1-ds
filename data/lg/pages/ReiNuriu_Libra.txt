Rei Nuriu (born 16 October 1998) is an Albanian professional footballer who plays as a defender for Albanian club Dinamo Tirana and the Albania national under-21 football team.

== International career ==
Nuriu received his first call up to the Albania national under-19 team by coach Arjan Bellaj for the friendly tournament Roma Caput Mundi from 29 February–4 March 2016.He received his first call up for the Albania under-20 side by same coach of the under-21 team Alban Bushi for the friendly match against Georgia U20 on 14 November 2017. He debuted for under-20 team against Georgia coming on as a substitute in the 55th minute for Shaqir Tafa in an eventual 3–0 loss.
