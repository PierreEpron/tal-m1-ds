Volodymyr Smarovoz (Ukrainian: Володимир Володимирович Смаровоз; 11 May 1956) is a former professional Soviet football defender and coach.
Smarovoz was one of the most played for SKA Odesa with 315 yielding only to Serhiy Marusyn.
