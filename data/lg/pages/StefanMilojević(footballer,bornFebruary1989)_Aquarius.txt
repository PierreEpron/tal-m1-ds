Stefan Milojević (born February 6, 1989) is a professional French-Serbian football player who currently plays for Woodlands Wellington as a midfielder.
Born in an area of former Yugoslavia (now Serbia), he was raised in southern France and spent most of his footballing career in France.


== Career ==
Milojević played for the youth teams of Solitaire Paris, AS Monaco and Birmingham City.His senior career was spent with Swiss club CS Chênois, as well as French lower league clubs ROS Menton and USC Corte. Milojević signed with Singapore's Geylang International in June 2012. He played with the Eagles for two seasons, before joining Woodlands Wellington in 2014.
