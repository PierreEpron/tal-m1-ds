Pedro Antonio Terán Mendoza (born July 24, 1996) is a Mexican footballer who currently plays for CAFESSA Jalisco, on loan from Atlas F.C.


== Career ==
Born in Saltillo, Terán began playing youth football with Necaxa Saltillo. He joined Club Atlas, where he would be loaned to Venados F.C. twice. He made his professional debut with Venados in the Ascenso MX.Terán played an important part in Mexico's participation at the 2013 FIFA U-17 World Cup. In December 2015 Terán joined Mérida club Venados F.C. on a six-month loan, a contract which was then extended.
