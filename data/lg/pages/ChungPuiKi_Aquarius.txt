Chung Pui Ki (born 2 February 1998) is a Hongkonger footballer who plays as a defender for Hong Kong Women League club Kitchee SC. She is also a futsal player, and represented Hong Kong internationally in both football and futsal.


== International career ==
Chung Pui Ki has been capped for Hong Kong at senior level in both football and futsal. In football, she represented Hong Kong at the 2013 AFC U-16 Women's Championship, two AFC U-19 Women's Championship editions (2015 and 2017), the 2018 Asian Games and the 2020 AFC Women's Olympic Qualifying Tournament.She scored her first international goal against India on 21 January 2019 in a friendly match.
In futsal, Chung Pui Ki played for Hong Kong at the 2015 AFC Women's Futsal Championship.
