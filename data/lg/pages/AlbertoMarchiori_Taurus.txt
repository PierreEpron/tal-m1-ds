Alberto Marchiori (born 11 May 1993) is an Italian footballer who plays as a centre back.In summer 2013 Marchiori left for Vicenza Calcio, with youngster Alessio Benedetti moved to opposite direction.
In July 2021, Alberto Marchiori signed with A.C. Este to play in Serie D, which is the fourth tier of the Italian league system.
