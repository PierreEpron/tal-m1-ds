Esther Elizabeth Blackie (née Ferguson; 29 January 1916 – 9 March 1991) was a New Zealand cricketer who played as a wicket-keeper and right-handed batter. She played in one Test match for New Zealand in 1949. She played domestic cricket for Otago.
