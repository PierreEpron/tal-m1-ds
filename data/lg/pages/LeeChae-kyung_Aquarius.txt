Lee Chae-kyung (Korean: 이채경; born on 22 January 1975) is a South Korean actress. She made her acting debut in 1998 in theater, and since then, she has appeared in a number of plays, films and television series. She got recognition for her supporting roles in Hotel del Luna, The Crowned Clown (2019), Love Scene Number, Jirisan and Moonshine (2021). She has acted in films such as Overman (2015) and #ALIVE (2020) among others.


== Career ==
Lee Chae-kyung has had an exclusive contract with artist management company YK Media Plus since August 2021.She was active as a musical actor in the Seoul Arts Troupe (2000-2007). In 2016, she appeared in the TV series Love in the Moonlight, Guardian: The Lonely and Great God and Dr. Romantic.


== Personal life and education ==
Lee Chae-kyung was Born in Sinjeong-dong, Nam-gu, Ulsan. She graduated from JoongAng Girls' Middle and High School, University of Ulsan, and then graduated in Performing Arts from Dankook University's graduate school.
