Brian Campman (born 12 December 1999) is a Dutch football player who plays for Oss '20.


== Club career ==
He made his professional debut in the Eerste Divisie for Achilles '29 on 18 November 2016 in a game against FC Oss.In the summer 2017, Campman joined NEC. He started out at the U19 squad.
