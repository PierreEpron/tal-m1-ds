Nikita Ilyich Chaly (Russian: Никита Ильич Чалый; born 7 May 1996) is a Russian football player.


== Club career ==
He made his debut in the Russian Second Division for FC Chernomorets Novorossiysk on 12 July 2013 in a game against FC Krasnodar-2.He made his Russian Football National League debut for FC Chayka Peschanokopskoye on 20 July 2019 in a game against FC Baltika Kaliningrad.
