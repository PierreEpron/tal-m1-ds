Nicolás Abot (born September 28, 1985 in Mar del Plata, Argentina) is an Argentine footballer.


== Career ==
Nicolás Abot joined the Latvian Higher League club FC Jūrmala in July 2014. Under the management of former Manchester United player Andrei Kanchelskis he played 10 league matches and scored 3 goals. He joined Persebaya on December 9, 2014.In 2017, Abot hat spells with Maltese club Victoria Wanderers and Italian Promozione club Atletico Tricase.
