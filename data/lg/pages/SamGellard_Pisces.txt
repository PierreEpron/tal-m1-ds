Sam Gellard (born March 15, 1950 in Port of Spain, Trinidad and Tobago) is a retired Canadian ice hockey left winger. He played 28 games for the Philadelphia/Vancouver Blazers of the World Hockey Association (WHA). As a youth, he played in the 1962 Quebec International Pee-Wee Hockey Tournament with his team from Don Mills, Toronto.Gellard's daughter Kim is a former World Curling champion.
