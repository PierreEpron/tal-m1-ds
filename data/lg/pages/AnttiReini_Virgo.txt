Antti Reini (born 27 August 1964) is a Finnish film actor. At the 27th Guldbagge Awards he was nominated for the award for Best Actor for his role in Il Capitano: A Swedish Requiem.Seven films (2011–2015) made from the Vares novels by Reijo Mäki feature Antti Reini; the first six of these (2011–2012) have been released on DVD in Region 1.
