Slaven Juriša (born 18 January 1992) is a Croatian footballer who currently plays for Tekstilac Odžaci.


== Club career ==
On 3 March 2017, he joined Motor Lublin on loan until the end of the 2016–17 season.After less than a half year at Serbian club FK Mladost Omoljica, Juriša joined Polish club Legionovia Legionowo in the summer 2018. He played 14 games and scored two goals in all competitions, before he left and joined FK Dinamo Pančevo at the end of January 2019.He was part of the "Croats from Serbia" team at the 2016 EUROPEADA games.
