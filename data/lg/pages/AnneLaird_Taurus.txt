Anne "Annie" Laird (born 20 May 1970) is a Scottish curler.In 1991, Laird won a bronze medal at the World Junior Curling Championships playing lead for Gillian Barr.
In 2002, Laird was a member of the gold medal-winning team at the World Curling Championships, playing lead for Jackie Lockhart.Laird replaced Karen Addison as the alternate in the British curling team for the 2010 Winter Olympic Games, after Addison was deselected.
