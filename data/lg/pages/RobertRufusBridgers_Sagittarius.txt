== Biography ==
Bridgers was born in Edgecombe County, North Carolina on November 28, 1819. He graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 1841. He served in the state legislature in 1844, and again from 1858 to 1861.He married Margaret Elizabeth Johnston on December 12, 1849.He represented the state in the First Confederate Congress and the Second Confederate Congress from 1862 to 1865.He died in Columbia, South Carolina on December 10, 1888.
