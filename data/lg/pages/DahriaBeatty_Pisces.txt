Dahria Beatty (born 7 March 1994) is a Canadian,  former cross-country skier who competed internationally.


== Career ==
She competed for Canada at the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships 2017 in Lahti, Finland, at the 2018 Winter Olympics, and at the 2019 World Championships.On January 13, 2022, Beatty was officially named to Canada's 2022 Olympic team.She announced her retirement from cross-country skiing on March 14, 2023.
