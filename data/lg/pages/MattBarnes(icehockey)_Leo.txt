Matt Barnes (born July 25, 1974) is a Canadian former professional ice hockey goaltender. He is currently a goalie coach with the Rick Heinz Goalie & Hockey School.Barnes attended Western Michigan University where he played four seasons of NCAA college hockey with the Western Michigan Broncos men's ice hockey team.
