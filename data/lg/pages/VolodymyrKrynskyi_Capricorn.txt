Volodymyr Viktorovych Krynskyi (Ukrainian: Володимир Вікторович Кринський; born 14 January 1997) is a Ukrainian professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for 1. SK Prostějov.


== Career ==
Krynskyi is a product of UFK-Olimpik Kharkiv youth sportive system. In 2014, he signed contract with FC Poltava in the Ukrainian First League and made his debut in match against FC Kolos Kovalivka on 8 October 2016.On 2 July 2018, he signed 3-year contract with Ukrainian Premier League club Olimpik Donetsk after the dissolution of FC Poltava.
