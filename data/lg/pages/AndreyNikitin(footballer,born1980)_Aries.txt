Andrei Mikhailovich Nikitin (Russian: Андрей Михайлович Никитин; born 24 March 1980) is a former Russian professional footballer.


== Club career ==
He made his debut in the Russian Premier League in 2002 for FC Torpedo-ZIL Moscow.
