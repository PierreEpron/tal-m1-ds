Elefthérios Eleftheríou (born June 12, 1974 in Larnaca, Cyprus) is a Cypriot football midfielder who played for Alki Larnaca. He also played for Enosis Neon Paralimni and AEK Larnaca.


== Honours ==
AEK Larnaca

Cypriot Cup: 2003–04
