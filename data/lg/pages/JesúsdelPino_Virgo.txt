Jesús del Pino Corrochano (born 9 September 1990 in Segurilla) is a Spanish cyclist, who currently rides for UCI Continental team Aviludo–Louletano–Loulé Concelho.


== Major results ==
2009
3rd Overall Vuelta a la Comunidad de Madrid U23
2013
1st Stage 2 Tour des Pays de Savoie
2014
3rd Overall Tour des Pays de Savoie
6th Overall Vuelta a Castilla y León
2017
1st  Overall Volta Internacional Cova da Beira
9th Overall Vuelta a Castilla y León
2019
2nd Prueba Villafranca de Ordizia
