Emir Sinanović (born 24 October 1988) is a Bosnian–Swiss footballer, who currently plays for Rothrist.


== Club career ==
After playing at the highest level in Switzerland with Aarau, he had two spells with Baden and played for FC Naters and Young Fellows Juventus.Sinanović also played for lower league sides Muri and Schötz and he joined Rothrist in summer 2020.
