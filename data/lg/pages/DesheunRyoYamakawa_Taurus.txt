Desheun Ryo Yamakawa (山川 デション諒, Yamakawa Desheun Ryō, born April 21, 1997) is a Japanese football player. He plays for FC Ryukyu.


== Career ==
Desheun Ryo Yamakawa joined J3 League club FC Ryukyu in 2016.
