Miodrag Gemović (Serbian Cyrillic: Миодраг Гемовић; born 25 December 1994) is a Serbian professional footballer, playing as a forward for Saudi Arabian club Al-Riyadh.
On 28 August 2021, Gemović joined Saudi Arabian club Al-Khaleej. On 14 June 2022, Gemović joined Al-Riyadh.
