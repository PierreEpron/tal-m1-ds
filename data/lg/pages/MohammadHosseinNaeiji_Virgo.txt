Mohammad Hossein Naeiji (Persian: محمد حسین نائیجی), is an Iranian goalkeeper who currently plays for Fajr Sepasi Shiraz F.C. in the Azadegan League. He previously played for Persepolis and Esteghlal F.C. in the Iran Pro League.


== Club career ==
He moved to Persepolis in summer 2009 from Esteghlal.
