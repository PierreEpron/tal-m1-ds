Fridrikh Mikhailovich Maryutin (Russian: Фридрих Михайлович Марютин) (October 7, 1924 – September 9, 2010) was a Soviet football player and manager. He was born in Astrakhan and died in Saint Petersburg. Maryutin played his only game for USSR on July 20, 1952 in a 1952 Olympics game against Yugoslavia.
