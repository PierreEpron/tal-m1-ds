Vladimir Vladimirovich Kuzmichyov (Russian: Владимир Владимирович Кузьмичёв; 28 July 1979 – 24 September 2016) was a Russian footballer.
He was a pupil of FC Spartak Moscow. In his rookie season  he played for Chernomorets Novorossiysk.On 24 September 2016 he died in a traffic accident.
