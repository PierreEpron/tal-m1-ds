Mai Uemura (上村 麻衣 Uemura Mai, born September 15, 1987) is a Japanese volleyball player who plays for Shikoku Eighty 8 Queen.


== Clubs ==
Tsushogyo High School → Denso Airybees (2006–2009) → Shikoku Eighty 8 Queen (2009-)
