Dragan Mićanović (Serbian: Драган Мићановић; born 30 September 1970) is a Serbian actor. He had roles in the films Layer Cake (2004), The White Countess (2005) and RocknRolla (2008). Mićanović played his first role abroad in Hamlet at the Globe Theatre.


== Personal life ==
Mićanović was married to Serbian actress Ana Sofrenović and they have two daughters, Iva and Lena. The family lived in London but they continued to work in Serbia and the Balkans. They divorced in November 2011.
