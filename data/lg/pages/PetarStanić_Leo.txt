Petar Stanić (Serbian Cyrillic: Петар Станић; born 14 August 2001) is a Serbian football attacking midfielder who plays for Spartak Subotica, on loan from Red Star Belgrade.


== Club career ==


=== Red Star Belgrade ===
Stanić signed a four-years contract with Red Star on 3 July 2021. He made a debut for the club on 29 August 2021 coming in as a substitute in a 1–2 away win against Čukarički.
