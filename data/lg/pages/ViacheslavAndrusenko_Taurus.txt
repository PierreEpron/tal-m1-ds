Viacheslav Dmitrievich Andrusenko (Russian: Вячеслав Дмитриевич Андрусенко; born 14 May 1992) is a Russian swimmer. He competed in the men's 4 × 200 metre freestyle relay event at the 2016 Summer Olympics.
