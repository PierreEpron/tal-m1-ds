Vitaliy Mykolayovych Ponomarenko (Ukrainian: Віталій Миколайович Пономаренко; Russian: Виталий Николаевич Пономаренко; born 23 January 1969) is a former Ukrainian professional footballer.


== Club career ==
He made his professional debut in 1988 for FC Dynamo Kyiv in the USSR Federation Cup.


== Honours ==
Ukrainian Premier League champion: 1993, 1994.
Ukrainian Cup winner: 1993.
