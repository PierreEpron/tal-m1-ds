Chris Lee Chih-cheng (Chinese: 李至正; pinyin: Lǐ Zhì Zhèng; Pe̍h-ōe-jī: Lí Chì-chèng; born November 11, 1981) is a Taiwanese actor and model.In 2004, he acted in Singapore's first action crime thriller film re:solve directed by Randy Ang.
