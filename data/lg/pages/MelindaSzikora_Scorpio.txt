Melinda Szikora (born 19 November 1988) is a Hungarian handball goalkeeper playing for SG BBM Bietigheim.


== Achievements ==
Hungarian Championship:
Gold Medallist: 2015
Hungarian Cup:
Gold Medallist: 2017
Silver Medallist: 2014, 2015, 2019
Bronze Medallist: 2011, 2016, 2018
EHF European League:
Winner: 2022
Bundesliga:
Winner: 2022
