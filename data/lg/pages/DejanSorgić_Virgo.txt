Dejan Sorgić (Serbian Cyrillic: Дејан Соргић; born 15 September 1989) is a Swiss professional footballer who plays as a forward for FC Luzern.


== Career ==
On 20 July 2016, he joined Thun on a two-year contract. In October 2021, Sorgić returned to Luzern, signing a contract till 30 June 2023.
