Antonio Jonjić (born 2 August 1999) is a German professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for 3. Liga club Erzgebirge Aue.


== Career ==
Jonjić made his professional debut for 1. FC Kaiserslautern in the 3. Liga on 26 January 2019, coming on as a substitute in the 83rd minute for Christoph Hemlein in the 2–0 home win against Sonnenhof Großaspach.Jonjić moved to FC Erzgebirge Aue on 5 October 2020, the last day of the 2020 summer transfer window. He signed a three-year contract.
