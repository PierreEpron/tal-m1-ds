Pradeep Trimbak Rawat (b 1956) (Marathi: प्रदीप त्रिंबक रावत) is an Indian politician from the Bharatiya Janata Party and a former Member of Parliament (MP), elected from Pune (Lok Sabha constituency) in 1999 to the 13th Lok Sabha. He contested from Pune again in 2004 but lost to Suresh Kalmadi of Congress.
