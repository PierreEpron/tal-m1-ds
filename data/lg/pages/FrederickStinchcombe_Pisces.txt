Stinchcombe made his first-class debut for Nottinghamshire against Hampshire in the 1950 County Championship.  He made five further first-class appearances for the county, the last of which came against Kent in the 1951 County Championship.  In his six first-class appearances for the county, he scored 87 runs at an average of 14.50, with a high score of 48.  With the ball, he took 4 wickets at an expensive bowling average of 134.75, with best figures of 1/42.He died at Worksop, Nottinghamshire, on 19 September 1984.
