Rezo Davidovich Gavtadze (Russian: Резо Давидович Гавтадзе; born 11 July 1995) is a Georgian former professional football player who played as a left midfielder. He also holds Russian citizenship.


== Club career ==
He made his professional debut on 19 March 2016 for FC Ural Sverdlovsk Oblast in a Russian Premier League game against FC Terek Grozny.
