Luiz Felipe Veloso Santos (born April 7, 1997), known as Lipe Veloso, is a Brazilian football player. He plays for Torpedo-BelAZ Zhodino.


== Career ==
Lipe Veloso joined J1 League club FC Tokyo in 2017.
