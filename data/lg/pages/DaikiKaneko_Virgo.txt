Daiki Kaneko (金子大毅, Kaneko Daiki, born August 28, 1998)  is a Japanese professional footballer who plays as a defensive midfielder for Kyoto Sanga, on loan from Urawa Red Diamonds.


== Career ==
After attending Kanagawa University, Kaneko joined Shonan Bellmare for 2018 season.
