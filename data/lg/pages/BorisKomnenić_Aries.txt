Boris Komnenić (Serbian Cyrillic: Борис Комненић; 29 March 1957 – 6 March 2021) was a Serbian actor. He appeared in more than sixty films from 1980 onwards and a number of theatre roles.Komnenić died on 6 March 2021 in Belgrade, aged 63.
