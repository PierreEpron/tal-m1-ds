Vinzenz Geiger (born 24 July 1997) is a German Nordic combined skier and the 2022 Olympic champion.He debuted in the World Cup in the 2015–16 season in Lillehammer, Norway in December 2016 finishing 31st in the Large Hill. His first win in the World Cup came in Val di Fiemme, in the 2018–19 season, in January 2019.
