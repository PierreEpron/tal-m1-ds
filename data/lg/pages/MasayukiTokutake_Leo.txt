Masayuki Tokutake (徳武 正之, Tokutake Masayuki, born August 18, 1991) is a Japanese football player. He plays for Azul Claro Numazu.


== Playing career ==
Masayuki Tokutake played for Zweigen Kanazawa from 2014 to 2015. In 2016, he moved to Azul Claro Numazu.
