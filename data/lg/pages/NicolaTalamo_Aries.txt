Nicola Talamo (born 8 April 1996) is an Italian footballer who plays for Nocerina in Serie D.


== Club career ==
Born in Pozzuoli, in the Province of Naples, Talamo started his career at local side Monteruscello Calcio. In 2013 he left for Serie D club Sora on loan. In 2014 Talamo was signed by Serie B club Latina, initially on loan.
