Ekaterina Olegovna Efremenkova (Russian: Екатерина Олеговна Ефременкова; born 31 December 1997) is a Russian short track speed skater. She won a bronze medal in the 3,000 meters relay at the 2016 World Short Track Speed Skating Championships.
