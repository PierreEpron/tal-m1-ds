Franz Freiherr von Zedlitz und Leipe (21 April 1876 – 29 March 1944) was a German Freiherr and sport shooter who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics.He won the bronze medal in the clay pigeons team event. He also competed in the Shooting at the 1912 Summer Olympics – Men's trap competition and finished 4th.
