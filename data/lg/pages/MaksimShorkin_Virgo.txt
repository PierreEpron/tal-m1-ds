Maksim Viktorovich Shorkin (Russian: Максим Викторович Шоркин; born 3 September 1990) is a Russian professional football player. He plays for FC Neftekhimik Nizhnekamsk.


== Club career ==
He made his debut in the Russian Football National League for FC KAMAZ Naberezhnye Chelny on 26 March 2012 in a game against FC Baltika Kaliningrad.
