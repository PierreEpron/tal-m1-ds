Aleksej Nešović (Serbian: Алексеј Нешовић; born March 14, 1985) is a Bosnian professional basketball player. Standing at 1.89 m (6 ft 2+1⁄2 in) he plays at the point guard position. He also represented the Bosnia and Herzegovina national basketball team internationally.
