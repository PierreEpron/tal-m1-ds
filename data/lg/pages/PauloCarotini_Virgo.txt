Paulo Kesselring Carotini (10 September 1945 – 10 February 2022), also known as Polé, was a Brazilian water polo player. He competed in the men's tournament at the 1964 Summer Olympics.Carotini died on 10 February 2022, at the age of 76. His brother is Ivo Carotini.
