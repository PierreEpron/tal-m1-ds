David Leslie Robson (born 22 January 2002) is a professional footballer who plays for Championship side Hull City as a goalkeeper and the Wales under-21 national team.


== Club career ==


=== Hull City ===
On 26 March 2022, Robson went on loan to National League North side Farsley Celtic for the remainder of their season.  There Robson made eight appearances and helped the club avoid relegation.
