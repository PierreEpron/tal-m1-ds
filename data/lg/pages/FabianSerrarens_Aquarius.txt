Fabian Serrarens (born 9 February 1991) is a Dutch professional footballer who plays as a striker. He formerly played for Arka Gdynia, De Graafschap, Telstar, Almere City FC, Roda JC Kerkrade and HJK.


== Club career ==
On 6 January 2022, he signed a one-year contract with HJK in Finland.
