Gerson Alexis Barreto Gamboa (born 18 August 1995) is a Peruvian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Primera División club Universitario.


== Career ==
Born in Lima, Barreto played youth football for Universitario before making his senior debut in 2013.Having played for Universitario and Academia Deportiva Cantolao, Barreto returned to Universitario in July 2019. He signed a two-year extension to his contract at Universitario in January 2021.
