Theodor Niederbach (born 25 February 2002) is a Swedish ice hockey forward currently playing for MoDo Hockey in the second-highest Swedish ice-hockey division, HockeyAllsvenskan. Niederbach was drafted 51st overall by the Detroit Red Wings in the 2020 NHL Entry Draft. He made his professional debut with Frölunda HC during the 2020–21 season.
