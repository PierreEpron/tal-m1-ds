Ryujiro Yamanaka (山中 隆二郎, Yamanaka Ryūjirō, born 24 September 1995 in Osaka) is a Japanese footballer who plays for Okinawa SV as a midfielder.


== Career ==
He signed for Albirex Niigata (S) after graduating from Osaka Sangyo University.He scored his first goal for the White Swan in the match against Geylang International on 28/4/2018.He scored another brace against Tampines Rovers in the match which the White Swan won 4–3.
