Eran Biton (Hebrew: ערן ביטון; born 16 January 1996) is an Israeli footballer who plays as a central midfielder. He currently plays for Beitar Tel Aviv Bat Yam.


== Early life ==
Biton was born in Ramat Gan, Israel, to a family of Sephardic Jewish descent.
