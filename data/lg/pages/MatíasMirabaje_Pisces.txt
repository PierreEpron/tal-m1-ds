Claudio Matías Mirabaje Correa (born March 6, 1989, in Montevideo) is a Uruguayan footballer currently playing as an attacking midfielder for Penya Encarnada d'Andorra in Primera Divisió .


== International career ==
Mirabaje has played for the Uruguay under-20 team at the 2009 FIFA U-20 World Cup in Egypt.
