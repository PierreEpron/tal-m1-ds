Mohd Arfan bin Rashid (born 5 March 1991, in Alor Setar, Kedah) is a Malaysian football midfielder currently playing for Kedah FA.Arfan, a member of the team that came runner-up in the 2009 Kings' Gold Cup (Malay: Piala Emas Raja-Raja), was promoted to the senior squad after Kedah coach Haji Ahmad Yusof brought him in as backup for current midfielder players. Ahmad was forced to bring another midfielder after Ahmad Fauzi Saari faced with a serious knee injury.
