Emil Lindholm (born 19 July 1996) is a Finnish rally driver. He is the son of former World Rally Championship driver Sebastian Lindholm. He is also the reigning champion of the FIA WRC2 World Rally Championship and he won the Finnish Rally Championship in 2021.
