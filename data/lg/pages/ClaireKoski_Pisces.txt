Claire Jennifer Koski (born 13 March 1991) is an Australian cricketer who plays for the ACT Meteors and the Melbourne Renegades.  She has previously played for the New South Wales Breakers and Sydney Thunder, and was a member of Australia's 2014 World Cup-winning women's indoor cricket team  .
