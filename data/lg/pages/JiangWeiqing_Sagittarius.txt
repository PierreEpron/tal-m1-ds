Jiang Weiqing (Chinese: 江渭清) (November 26, 1910 – June 16, 2000) was a People's Republic of China politician. He was born in Pingjiang County, Hunan Province. He was Chinese Communist Party Committee Secretary and CPPCC Committee Chairman of Jiangsu Province and Chinese Communist Party Committee Secretary of Jiangxi Province.
