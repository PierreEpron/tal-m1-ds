Everett Lee "Skeeter" Kell  (October 11, 1929 – May 28, 2015) was a Major League Baseball second baseman. 
The younger brother of Hall of Famer George Kell, Skeeter played college baseball at the Arkansas Razorbacks baseball from 1948–1951. He played one major league season, 1952, for the Philadelphia Athletics, splitting time at second base with Cass Michaels and Pete Suder.
