Roberto Secundino Digón (25 July 1935 – 30 January 2022) was an Argentine politician and sports executive.


== Biography ==
A member of the Justicialist Party, he served in the Argentine Chamber of Deputies from 1985 to 1989 and again from 1993 to 1997.He died of COVID-19 in Buenos Aires on 30 January 2022, at the age of 86.
