Aaron Mattia Tabacchi (born 26 June 1998) is an Italian football player.


== Club career ==
He made his professional debut in the Serie A for Bologna on 15 May 2016 as an 83rd-minute substitute for Emanuele Giaccherini in a game against Chievo.On 31 January 2019, he was loaned to Imolese.
