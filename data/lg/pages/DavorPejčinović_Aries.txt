Davor Pejčinović (born 28 March 1971) is a retired Croatian basketball player. A 2.11 m center, he participated in the 2000-01 Euroleague with KK Zadar.Pejčinović was a member of the Croatian national team that won the bronze medal in the 1995 Eurobasket.
