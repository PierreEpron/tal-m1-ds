Taylon Nicolas Correa Marcolino (born 16 March 1995), or simply Taylon,  is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Kuwaiti Division One club Burgan.


== Career ==
On 25 June 2019, Taylon Correa signed a two-year contract with Botev Plovdiv, playing five times for the club before leaving by mutual consent on 26 August 2019.On 18 September 2019 Taylon Correa confirmed, that he had joined Al Urooba in the United Arab Emirates.
