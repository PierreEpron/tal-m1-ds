Thomas Rath (born 26 July 1970 in Schwedt) is a German former professional footballer who played as a midfielder for Hertha BSC, Dynamo Dresden, SC Freiburg and VfB Leipzig.He was a part of the East German squad at the 1989 FIFA World Youth Championship, playing two matches.
