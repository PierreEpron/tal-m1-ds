Luis Antonio Mendoza Castro (born August 21, 1973) is a Mexican football manager and former player.


== Career ==
Born in Villa Guerrero, State of Mexico, Mendoza made his professional debut with Deportivo Toluca F.C. in 1990.Mendoza became the manager of Ascenso MX side Murciélagos F.C. in 2017.
