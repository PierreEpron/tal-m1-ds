Felipe Francisco Macedo (born 27 March 1994) is a Brazilian footballer who plays as a centre-back. He is a free agent.


== Club career ==
Born in São Miguel do Araguaia, Goiás, Felipe Macedo graduated with Goiás' youth setup and made his senior debuts while on loan at Aparecidense. On 3 October 2013, he made his first team – and Série A – debut for the latter, coming on as a first-half substitute in a 1–2 away loss against Vitória.In 2014, Felipe Macedo was made a starter by manager Ricardo Drubscky. On 26 February 2015, he renewed his link with the club, signing until 2019.
