Geert Emmerechts (Vilvoorde, 5 May 1968) is a former Belgian footballer who played as central defender.


== Honours ==
Royal Antwerp

Belgian Cup: 1991-92
UEFA Cup Winners' Cup: 1992-93 (runners-up)
