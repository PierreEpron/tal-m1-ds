Gao Lundong (simplified Chinese: 高鲁东; traditional Chinese: 高魯東; pinyin: Gāo Lǔ Dōng; born April 18, 1960) is a Chinese sprint canoeist who competed in the mid-1980s. At the 1984 Summer Olympics in Los Angeles, he was eliminated in the repechages of the K-2 500 m event.
