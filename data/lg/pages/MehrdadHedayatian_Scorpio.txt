Mehrdad Hedayatian (Persian: مهرداد هراتیان, born 2 November 1997) is an Iranian footballer who plays as a winger who currently plays for Iranian club Naft Masjed Soleyman in the Persian Gulf Pro League.


== Club career ==


=== Foolad ===
He made his debut for Foolad in 2nd fixtures of 2017–18 Iran Pro League against Naft Tehran.
