Hu Jianguan (Chinese: 胡建关; pinyin: Hú Jiàn Guān; born 11 May 1993) is a Chinese male boxer. He won the bronze medal in the men's 52 kg at the 2016 Olympics in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
