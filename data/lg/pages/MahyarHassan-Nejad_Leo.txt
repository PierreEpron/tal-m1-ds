Mahyar Hassan-Nejad (Persian: مهیار حسن نژاد, August 6, 1985 in Sari, Iran) is an Iranian footballer who currently plays for Sanat Sari in the Azadegan League.


== Club career ==
Hassan-Nejad joined Sanat Sari in 2010–11 season.
