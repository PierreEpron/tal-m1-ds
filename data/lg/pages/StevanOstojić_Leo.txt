Stevan Ostojić (Serbian Cyrillic: Стеван Остојић; 20 August 1941 – 15 May 2022) was a Serbian professional footballer who played as a striker.Born just a few months following the start of World War II in Yugoslavia to father Spasoje who worked as a railroad engineer, young Ostojić grew up in Subotica where the family moved during the war. He began playing football during the mid-1950s when his older brother Ivan who already played with OFK Subotica took him to a training session.


== Honours ==
Red Star Belgrade

Yugoslav First League: 1967–68, 1968–69
Yugoslav Cup: 1967–68, 1970–71
Mitropa Cup: 1967–68
