Thomas Denis (born 18 July 1997) is a French track and road cyclist, who currently rides for UCI Continental team Go Sport–Roubaix–Lille Métropole. Representing France at international competitions, Denis won the gold medal at the 2016 UEC European Track Championships in the team pursuit.
