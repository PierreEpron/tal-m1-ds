Benjamin Thomas Wyatt (born 4 February 1996) is an English professional footballer who plays as a left back for Torquay United. He will become a free agent on 30 June 2022.


== Career ==
After playing youth football with Norwich City and Ipswich Town, and in non-league for Maldon & Tiptree, Wyatt signed a one-year professional contract with Colchester United in May 2016. He moved on loan to Concord Rangers in October 2016. He moved to Braintree Town in August 2017. After playing for St Albans City, he signed for Sutton United in September 2019.
