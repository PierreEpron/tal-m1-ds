Vincent Marshall Gavre (April 5, 1914 – December 25, 2006) was an American football player and coach. He was drafted in the tenth round of the 1939 NFL Draft. He served as the head football coach at Western State College of Colorado—now known as Western Colorado University—in Gunnison, Colorado from 1948 to 1949, compiling a record of 11–7.
