Feng Yu (Chinese: 冯雨, born 30 September 1999) is a Chinese synchronised swimmer. She won a gold medal (China's first ever) and 2 silver medals at the 2017 World Aquatics Championships. She also won 4 silver medals at the 2016 Asian Swimming Championships.
