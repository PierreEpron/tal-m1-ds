Prasid Jantum (Thai: ประสิทธิ์ จันทุม, born April 30, 1995) is a Thai professional footballer who plays as a central midfielder for Thai League 1 club PT Prachuap.


== International career ==
Prasid was part of Thailand U19's squad in the 2014 Hassanal Bolkiah Trophy.
