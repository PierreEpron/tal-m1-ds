Jelena Karličić (born 5 October 2002) is a Montenegrin footballer who plays as a forward for Bordeaux and has appeared for the Montenegro women's national team.


== Career ==
Karličić has been capped for the Montenegro national team, appearing for the team during the UEFA Women's Euro 2021 qualifying cycle.
