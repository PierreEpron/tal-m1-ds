Andrew Zinni  (born 6 March 1965) is an Australian former international soccer player who most notably played for Brunswick Juventus and Preston Macedonia in the National Soccer League (NSL). Zinni played 17 times for the Australia national soccer team, including 10 times in full international matches.Andrew's son, Stefan Zinni, is also a professional footballer.
