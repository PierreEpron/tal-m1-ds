Rintaro Tashima (田嶋 凜太郎, Tashima Rintarō, born 8 April 1997) is a Japanese football player who last played for Dinamo Zagreb II.


== Club career ==
He made his professional debut in the Eerste Divisie for VVV-Venlo on 24 February 2017 in a game against Achilles '29.
