Jan Ščotka (born May 20, 1996) is a Czech professional ice hockey player. He is currently playing for HC Litvínov of the Czech Extraliga.Ščotka made his Czech Extraliga debut playing with HC Pardubice during the 2014–15 Czech Extraliga season.
