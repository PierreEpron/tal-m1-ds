Claude Dubaële (born 19 January 1940) is a French former professional football player and coach. He played for Stade Reims, Rennes, Angers SCO, Lille OSC and Le Mans. He was also part of France's squad at the 1960 Summer Olympics.After retiring as a player, Dubaële enjoyed a career as a manager with Fossemange, Rennes and Red Star 93.
