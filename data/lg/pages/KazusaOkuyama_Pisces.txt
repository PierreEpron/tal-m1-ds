Kazusa Okuyama (奥山 かずさ, Okuyama Kazusa, born March 10, 1994 in Misawa, Aomori) is a Japanese actress and model who is affiliated with Oscar Promotion and MOC Planning. She is a Miyagi University of Education graduate.
