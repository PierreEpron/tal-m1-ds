Roman Furger (born 10 February 1990) is a Swiss, former cross-country skier who represented the club Schattdorf.He competed at the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships 2017 in Lahti, Finland.
He announced his retirement from cross-country skiing on March 8, 2023.
