Muhammad Shaban (born 11 January 1998) is a Ugandan international footballer who plays for Onduparaka, as a striker.


== Club career ==
Born in Arua, Shaban signed a contract with Vipers in 2015, but it was rendered void and he signed for Onduparaka instead. He signed a three-year contract with Kampala Capital City Authority in October 2017, scoring on his debut a month later. In August 2018 he was linked with transfer to South African club Orlando Pirates and Moroccan club Raja Casablanca. == International career ==
He made his international debut in 2016, and was named in the squad for the 2017 Africa Cup of Nations.
