Murtazi Dzheyranovich Alakhverdov (Russian: Муртази Джейранович Алахвердов; born 12 September 1980) is a Russian professional football player. He plays for FC Sakhalin Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk.


== Club career ==
He made his Russian Football National League debut for FC Sakhalin Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk on 6 July 2014 in a game against FC Anzhi Makhachkala.
