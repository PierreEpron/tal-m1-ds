Carl Wilkinson (born 9 May 1984) is an English professional darts player playing in Professional Darts Corporation events.He attended UK Q-School in 2019, where he won a two-year Tour Card after finishing 2nd on the Order of Merit rankings.
He attributes much of his success to his mentor and hero Dave "The Thud" Calvert.
