Peyton Alex Smith (born June 18, 1994) is an American actor, best known for his role as Damon Sims in the TV series All American: Homecoming as well as Rafael Waithe in the CW series Legacies and  Cedric Hobbs in the TV series The Quad.Smith attended Florida A&M University
