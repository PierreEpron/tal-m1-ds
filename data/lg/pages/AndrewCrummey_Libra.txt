Andrew Crummey (born October 22, 1984) is a former American football guard. He was signed by the Washington Redskins as an undrafted free agent in 2008. He played college football at Maryland.Crummey was also a member of the Cincinnati Bengals, Houston Texans, Jacksonville Jaguars, Carolina Panthers, and Las Vegas Locomotives.


== Early years ==
Crummey graduated from Van Wert High School where he helped his team to a state championship game in 2000.
