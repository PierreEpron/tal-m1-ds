Mohammad Roknipour (Persian: محمد رکنی‌پور, born October 13, 1992) is an Iranian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Golden State Misioneros in the Premier Development League.


== Career ==
Roknipour signed his first professional contract with Los Angeles Blues at the tail end of their final season in the USL Pro in 2013. He later continued with the club with their move to Orange County and their re-branding to Orange County Blues in 2014.Prior to that, Roknipour played in the USL Premier Development League for OC Blues Strikers.
