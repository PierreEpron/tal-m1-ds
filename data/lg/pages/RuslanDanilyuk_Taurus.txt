Ruslan Danilyuk (Belarusian: Руслан Данілюк; Russian: Руслан Николаевич Данилюк; born 12 May 1974) is a retired Belarusian professional footballer.


== Honours ==
Slavia Mozyr

Belarusian Premier League champion: 2000
Belarusian Cup winner: 1999–2000
