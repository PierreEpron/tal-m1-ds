Rodrigo Pastorini de León (born 4 March 1990), commonly known as Rodrigo Pastorini, is a Uruguayan footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder or forward for Italian Serie C Group C club Fidelis Andria.


== Club career ==
On 31 January 2023, Pastorini signed with Fidelis Andria in the Italian third-tier Serie C.


== International career ==
Pastorini made his senior début for Uruguay at the 2007 South American Under-17 Football Championship.
