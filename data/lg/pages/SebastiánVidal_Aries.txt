Sebastián Vidal (born 18 April 1989, in Argentina) is an Argentine retired footballer.


== Career ==
After failing to make an appearance for Boca Juniors, Vidal played for Club Atlético Temperley, Estudiantes de Buenos Aires, as well as CA Excursionistas in the Argentine lower leagues.At the age of 31, he retired to become sports secretary of Avellaneda.
