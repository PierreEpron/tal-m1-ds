Masooma Hayat (Urdu: معسومہ حیات; born 15 March 1980) is a Pakistani politician who was a Member of the Provincial Assembly of Balochistan, from May 2013 to May 2018.


== Early life and education ==
Hayat was born on 15 March 1980 in Quetta, Balochistan, Pakistan.She has the degree of Master of Science and the degree of the Master of Education.


== Political career ==
She was elected to the Provincial Assembly of Balochistan as a candidate of Pashtunkhwa Milli Awami Party on a reserved seat for women in 2013 Pakistani general election.
