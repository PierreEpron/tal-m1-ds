Zhang Lei (Chinese: 張磊; pinyin: Zhāng Lěi; born 11 January 1985 in Shanghai), she was a member of the China women's national volleyball team who plays in the opposite position. She played for Shanghai in China.


== Club career ==
 Shanghai (2001-2013)
 Igtisadchi Baku (2013-2014)
 Shanghai (2014–2021)


== Awards ==


=== Clubs ===
2013–14 Azerbaijan Super League -  Bronze Medal, with Igtisadchi Baku
