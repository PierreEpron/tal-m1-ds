Danila Olegovich Smirnov (Russian: Данила Олегович Смирнов; born 7 June 2001) is a Russian football player. He plays as a central midfielder for Volgar Astrakhan.


== Club career ==
He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Krylia Sovetov-2 Samara on 13 October 2017 in a game against FC Volga Ulyanovsk.He made his debut for the senior squad of PFC Krylia Sovetov Samara on 25 September 2019 in a Russian Cup game against FC Torpedo Moscow. He made his Russian Premier League debut for Krylia Sovetov on 4 July 2020 in a game against FC Rostov, replacing Dmitri Kabutov in the 80th minute.
