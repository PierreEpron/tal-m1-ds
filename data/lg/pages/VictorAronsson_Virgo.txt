Victor Aronsson (born September 18, 1992) is a Swedish professional ice hockey defenceman currently playing for Almtuna IS of the HockeyAllsvenskan.Aronsson made his Swedish Hockey League debut playing with Karlskrona HK during the 2015-16 SHL season.
