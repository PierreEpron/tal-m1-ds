Emily Shearman (born 23 February 1999) is a New Zealand racing cyclist. She represented her country at the 2022 Commonwealth Games, winning a silver medal in the team pursuit.She competed at the 2019/2020 World Cup, and 2019/2020 Oceania Championships.
