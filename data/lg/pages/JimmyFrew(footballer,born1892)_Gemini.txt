== Career ==
Born in Kinghorn, Frew played for Kilsyth Emmet, Alloa Athletic, Newcastle City, Hearts, Leeds United and Bradford City.While at Hearts, he made one appearance for Scotland in an unofficial wartime international in 1916 along with club teammate Willie Wilson. By that time both men  had enlisted in 'McCrae's Battalion' of the Royal Scots, and Frew also served in the Royal Garrison Artillery during the conflict.For Leeds United he made 96 appearances in the Football League, plus three in the FA Cup.For Bradford City he made 48 appearances in the Football League.
