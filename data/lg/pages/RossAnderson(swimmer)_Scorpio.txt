Ross Woods Anderson (born 24 October 1968) is a former butterfly and freestyle swimmer for New Zealand
He competed for New Zealand at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea. There he was eliminated in the qualifying heats of the 100 m freestyle, and 100 m and 200 m butterfly.
