Anya Pencheva (Bulgarian: Аня Пенчева; born 12 September 1957) is a Bulgarian actress.


== Biography ==
She was born in Smolyan, but soon after that her family relocated to Troyan. Pencheva has performed on numerous occasions in the youth theater (Bulgarian: Младежки театър), which she describes as the best years in her life.Pencheva has been married twice - to sports journalist Sasho Dikov and Ivaylo Karanyotov.
