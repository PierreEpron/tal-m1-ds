Valdumar Augusto Té (born 14 August 1997) is a Guinea-Bissauan professional footballer who plays for Yanbian Longding as a forward.


== Club career ==
On 22 September 2018, Té made his professional debut with Vitória Setúbal in a 2018–19 Primeira Liga match against 
FC Porto.On 15 April 2023, Té signed with China League One side Yanbian Longding.
