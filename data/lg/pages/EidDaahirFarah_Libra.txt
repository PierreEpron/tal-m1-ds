Eid Daahir Faarah (Somali: Ciid Daahir Faarax) or Ciid (Somali: Ciid Daahir) is an Ethiopian politician who was the president of the  Somali Region of Ethiopia from 1995 to 1997. He served in the Somali regional parliament and was a member of the ruling Somali Democratic League, Ethiopian Somali Democratic League.


== Life ==
Eid was born in 1961, in Harshin, Ethiopia, then a part of Hawd and Reserve Area. He hails from the Sa'ad Musa sub-division of the Habar Awal clan of Isaaq.
