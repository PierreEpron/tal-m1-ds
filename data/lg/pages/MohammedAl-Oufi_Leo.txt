Mohammed Al-Oufi (Arabic: محمد العوفي; born 8 August 2002) is a Saudi Arabian footballer who plays as a left back for Saudi Pro League side Al-Adalah on loan from Al-Ittihad.


== Career ==
On 18 August 2022, Al-Oufi joined Pro League side Al-Adalah on a season long loan from Al-Ittihad.
