Sahawit Khumpiam (Thai: สหวิช ขำเปี่ยม; born 17 November 1994) is a Thai professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Thai League 1 club Lamphun Warriors.


== Personal life ==
Sahawit's father, Chaiyong Khumpiam, is a retired footballer who also played as a goalkeeper.
