Nikolay Nikolayevich Leonov (Russian: Никола́й Никола́евич Лео́нов; born 1 November 1955) is a Russian professional association football official and a former player.


== Club career ==
He spent one season in 1980 in the Soviet Top League for FC Lokomotiv Moscow.
