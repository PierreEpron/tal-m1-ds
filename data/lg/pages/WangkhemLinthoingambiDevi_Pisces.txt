Wangkhem Linthoingambi Devi (born 1 March 1995) is an Indian international footballer who plays as a left back for Kickstart and the India women's national team.


== Club career ==
Wangkhem has played for KRYPHSA in India.


== International career ==
Wangkhem capped for India at senior level during the 2019 South Asian Games.
