Ágnes Babos (12 May 1944 – 13 May 2020) was a Hungarian handball player and World champion.She was born in Kecskemét and died on 13 May 2020.


== Achievements ==
Nemzeti Bajnokság I:
Winner: 1972, 1973, 1974, 1975
Magyar Kupa:
Winner: 1965, 1969, 1971, 1974, 1976
World Championship:
Winner: 1965
Bronze Medalist: 1971


== Awards ==
Hungarian Handballer of the Year: 1970, 1972
