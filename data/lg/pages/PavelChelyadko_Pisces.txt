Pavel Chelyadko (Belarusian: Павел Чалядка; Russian: Павел Челядко; (born 3 March 1993) is a Belarusian professional footballer who plays as a full-back for Orlęta Radzyń Podlaski.


== Honours ==
Torpedo-BelAZ Zhodino

Belarusian Cup winner: 2015–16
