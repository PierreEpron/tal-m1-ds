== World tour 2016 ==
At the 2016 Grand Slam at Long Beach, California, 
In semi final action (Aug 27, 2016) Julia Sude and Laboureur lost to Kerri Walsh Jennings and April Ross of United States straight sets (21 - 17, 21 - 16).
Bronze medal match (Germany vs Germany) the pair won in straight sets (21-16, 21-17) against Katrin Holtwick and Ilka Semmler.The pair competed at the Toronto World Tour finals in Sept. 2016  placing 1st in Pool D and advance to Quarter Finals.
