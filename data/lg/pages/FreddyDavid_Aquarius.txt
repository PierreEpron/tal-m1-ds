Freddy David (Hebrew: פרדי דוד; born 21 January 1965) is an Israel former football player and manager.
He previously managed Hapoel Petah Tikva, Maccabi Herzliya, Bnei Sakhnin, Maccabi Petah Tikva, and Hapoel Ramat Gan.


== Honours ==
Toto Cup 2007
Toto Cup (Leumit) 2011
Liga Leumit 2011–12
