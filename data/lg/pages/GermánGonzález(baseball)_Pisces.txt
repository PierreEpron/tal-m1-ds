Germán José González (born March 7, 1962 in Río Caribe, Sucre State, Venezuela) is a former Major League Baseball relief pitcher who played for the Minnesota Twins (1988–89). He batted and threw right-handed.In a two-season career, González compiled a 3–2 record with 44 strikeouts and a 4.41 ERA in 50.1 innings.
