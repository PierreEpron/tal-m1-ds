Andrei Vladimirovich Razin (Russian: Андре́й Влади́мирович Ра́зин; born 23 October 1973), is a Russian former professional ice hockey player. Razin was selected by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 6th round (177th overall) of the 2001 NHL Entry Draft.
