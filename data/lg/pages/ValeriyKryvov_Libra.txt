Valeriy Mikhaylovych Kryvov (Ukrainian: Валерій Михайлович Кривов, 29 September 1951 – 20 December 1994) was a Ukrainian former volleyball player who competed for the Soviet Union in the 1980 Summer Olympics.
He was born in Krychaw, Byelorussian SSR.
In 1980, he was part of the Soviet team which won the gold medal in the Olympic tournament. He played all five matches.
