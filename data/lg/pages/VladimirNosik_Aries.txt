Vladimir Benediktovich Nosik (Russian: Влади́мир Бенеди́ктович Но́сик) is a Soviet and Russian actor, Honored Artist of Russia (1993), People's Artist of Russia (2016).Older brother —  People's Artist of Russia Valery Nosik, daughter —  actress Ekaterina Nosik.
