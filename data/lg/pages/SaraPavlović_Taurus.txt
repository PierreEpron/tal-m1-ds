Sara Pavlović (born 10 May 1996) is a Serbian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Spartak and has appeared for the Serbia women's national team.


== Career ==
Pavlović has been capped for the Serbia national team, appearing for the team during the 2019 FIFA Women's World Cup qualifying cycle.
