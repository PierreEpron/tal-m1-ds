Dragan Kojadinović (Serbian: Драган Којадиновић, born 29 November 1954) is a Serbian journalist and former Minister of Culture.


== Early life and education ==
He graduated from the Department of Yugoslav Literature and Serbian Language at the University of Belgrade Faculty of Philology.


== Career ==
For almost thirty years he has worked as a journalist, and was the first director of the Studio B independent television. in 1990. He is one of the founders of the Independent Serbian Journalist Association (NUNS), and is a director of the Metropolis television company and the President of the Serbian Renewal Movement Committee in Belgrade.In 2004 he unsuccessfully campaigned to become the mayor of Belgrade.


== Personal life ==
He speaks English. He is married with two children.
