Giacomo Casoli (born 15 September 1988) is an Italian professional football player who plays as a midfielder for Serie D club Casertana.


== Career ==
On 5 June 2012 Spezia bought the other half part of property by Fiorentina.On 12 September 2018, he was signed by Gubbio on a free transfer.On 31 January 2019, he signed with Catanzaro.On 25 August 2021, he joined Fidelis Andria.On 29 July 2022, Casoli moved to Casertana in Serie D.
