Petar Yordanov Velichkov (Bulgarian: Петьр Йорданов Величков; 8 August 1940 – 15 July 1993) was a Bulgarian football midfielder who played for Bulgaria in the 1962 FIFA World Cup. He also played for Slavia Sofia.
