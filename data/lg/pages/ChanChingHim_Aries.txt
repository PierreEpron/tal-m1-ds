Matthew Chan Ching Him (Chinese: 陳靜謙; born 18 April 1998) is a Hong Kong professional footballer who currently plays as a centre back for Hong Kong Premier League club Sham Shui Po.


== Club career ==
Chan made his professional debut for Southern on 3 December 2017 in a Sapling Cup match against Kitchee.On 20 October 2020, Chan was named as one of 17 new players for Pegasus.In August 2021, Chan joined HK U23.
On 8 August 2022, Chan joined Sham Shui Po.
