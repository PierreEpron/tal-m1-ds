Mohammad Soleiman Alizadeh (Persian: محمدسلیمان علیزاده; born 27 September 1991) is an Iranian footballer who plays as a midfielder.


== Club career ==
Alizadeh started his career as a youth player at Rah Ahan. He was promoted to the first team in the summer of 2012 by head coach Ali Daei.
