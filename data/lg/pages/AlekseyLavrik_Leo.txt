Aleksey Lavrik (Belarusian: Аляксей Лаўрык; Russian: Алексей Лаврик; born 7 August 2000) is a Belarusian footballer who plays for Energetik-BGU Minsk.He is a son of Belarusian coach and former international footballer Andrei Lavrik.
