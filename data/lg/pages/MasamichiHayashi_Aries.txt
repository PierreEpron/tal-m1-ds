Masamichi Hayashi (林　誠道, Hayashi Masamichi, born 4 April 1996) is a Japanese footballer who plays for Zweigen Kanazawa.


== Career ==
After five seasons in Tottori and a solid 2019, Hayashi signed with newly-promoted FC Imabari in January 2020.
