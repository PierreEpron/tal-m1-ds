Péter Marót (27 May 1945 – 7 June 2020) was a Hungarian fencer. He won a silver medal in the individual sabre and a bronze in the team event at the 1972 Summer Olympics.  He also competed at the 1976 Summer Olympics.Marót died on 7 June 2020 in a traffic collision.
