Elena Suslova is a former Russian football defender, who played for Zvezda Perm in the Russian Championship. She has won 5 championships with Energiya Voronezh and Zvezda.She has been a member of the Russian national team, but missed the 2009 European Championship due to an injury. She played during the 2009 European Championship qualification.


== Titles ==
5 Russian Leagues (2002, 2003, 2007, 2008, 2009)
2 Russian Cups (2001, 2007)
