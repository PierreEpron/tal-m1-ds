Huang Xiangdong (Chinese: 黄向东 born 22 March 1958 in Dalian) is a Chinese football coach and a former international player. In his football career he represented the Kunming Army Unit, Bayi Football Team and Locomotive while internationally he played for China in the 1980 Asian Cup and 1982 FIFA World Cup qualification campaign. Since retiring he has moved into coaching with his last appointment being with Chinese league two club Wenzhou Provenza in their 2010 league campaign.
