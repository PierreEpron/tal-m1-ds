Gordon Stephen "Gus" Giovanelli (born April 11, 1925) is an American former rower who competed in the 1948 Summer Olympics. He rowed for the University of Washington and  in 1948 he was the bowman of the American boat which won the gold medal in the coxed fours event. He was born in Everett, Washington.Giovanelli is the oldest surviving United States Olympic gold medalist.
