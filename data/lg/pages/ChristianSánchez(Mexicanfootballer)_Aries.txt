Christian Emmanuel Sánchez Narváez (born 4 April 1989) is a Mexican football defender who plays for Alebrijes de Oaxaca in Ascenso MX. Sánchez was a member of the 2005 FIFA U-17 World Championship squad that won the championship. Sánchez was transferred to Monarcas Morelia on 14 December 2009, but was released after the season.
