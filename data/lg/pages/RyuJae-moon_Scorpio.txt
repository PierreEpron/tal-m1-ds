Ryu Jae-moon (Korean: 류재문; born 8 November 1993) is a South Korean footballer who plays as a midfielder for Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors.


== Career ==
Born on 8 November 1993, Ryu signed with Daegu FC prior to the start of the 2015 season.On 11 January 2021, Ryu signed with Jeonbuk Hyundai Motors on a three-year deal.
