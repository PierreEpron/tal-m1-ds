Anton Bengtsson (born May 13, 1993) is a Swedish professional ice hockey forward currently playing with Rögle BK in the Swedish Hockey League (SHL). 


== Playing career ==
Bengtsson played two seasons in the J20 SuperElit with HV71 before he made his Elitserien debut with the senior squad during the 2012–13 Elitserien season.On 11 April 2019, Bengtsson left HV71 after seven seasons to sign as a free agent to a two-year contract with fellow SHL club, Rögle BK.
