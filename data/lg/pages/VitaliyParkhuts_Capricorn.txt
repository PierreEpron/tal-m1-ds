Vitaliy Volodymyrovych Parkhuts (Ukrainian: Віталій Володимирович Пархуць; born 18 January 1997) is a Ukrainian professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for Ukrainian club Prykarpattia Ivano-Frankivsk.
