Alina Grijseels  (born 12 April 1996) is a German handball player for Borussia Dortmund and the German national team.She participated at the 2018 European Women's Handball Championship.


== Achievements ==
Handball-Bundesliga Frauen:
Winner: 2021
DHB-Pokal:
Finalist: 2016
