Carmen Becerra  (born Carmen Alicia Becerra García December 7, 1977 in Toluca, State of Mexico, Mexico) is a Mexican actress best known for her antagonistic roles as Sara de la Cruz in Querida Enemiga (2008), Adriana in Zacatillo, un lugar en tu corazón (2010) and Marissa in Amorcito Corazón (2011).Becerra has starred in ten telenovela productions in Mexico.


== Personal life ==
Carmen wanted to be a chef, but she became an actress. She married very young, but she is divorced from her husband and now remarried.
