Patrick Shanahan (10 March 1908 – 1 February 2000) was an Irish Fianna Fáil politician.He was elected to Dáil Éireann as a Fianna Fáil Teachta Dála (TD) for the Clare constituency at the December 1945 by-election to succeed Patrick Burke. He lost his seat at the 1948 general election. He died in 2000, aged 91.
