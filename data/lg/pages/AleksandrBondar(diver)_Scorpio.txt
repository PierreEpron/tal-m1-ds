Aleksandr Igorevich Bondar (Ukrainian: Олександр Ігорович Бондар, Russian: Александр Игоревич Бондарь, IPA: [ɐlʲɪˈksandr ˈbondərʲ]; born 25 October 1993) is a Ukrainian-born Russian diver.


== Career ==
He competed for Ukraine at the 2012 Summer Olympics in the men's synchronized 10 metre platform.Bondar moved to Russia at the end of 2014 to be closer to relatives and his Russian girlfriend, Yekaterina Fedorchenko. He and Fedorchenko married in January 2015. In October 2015, he became a Russian citizen.
