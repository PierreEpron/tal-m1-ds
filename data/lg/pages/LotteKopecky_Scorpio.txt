Lotte Kopecky (born 10 November 1995) is a Belgian road and track racing cyclist, who rides for UCI Women's WorldTeam SD Worx. She is a multiple world champion on the track, having won four gold medals across three UCI Track Cycling World Championships; she won the madison in 2017 and 2022, the points race in 2021 and the elimination race in 2022. She participated in the 2020 Summer Olympics in Tokyo, finishing 4th in the individual road race. In 2022, she won the Strade Bianche and Tour of Flanders classics.
