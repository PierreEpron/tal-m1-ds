Vitali Yuryevich Vodopyanov (Russian: Виталий Юрьевич Водопьянов; born 12 September 1974) is a former Russian professional football player.


== Club career ==
He made his Russian Football National League debut for FC Lada Togliatti on 27 June 1992 in a game against FC Rubin-TAN Kazan.
