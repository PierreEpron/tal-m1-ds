Vojtěch Král (born 4 July 1988) is a Czech orienteering competitor, and junior world champion.
He became Junior World Champion in sprint in Dubbo in 2007.He competed at the 2012 World Orienteering Championships. In the sprint competition he qualified for the final, where he placed 10th.
