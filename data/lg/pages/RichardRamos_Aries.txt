Richard Ramos is a French restaurant critic and politician of the Democratic Movement (MoDem) who has been serving as a member of the National Assembly since 18 June 2017, representing the department of Loiret.


== Early career ==
Before entering politics full-time, Ramos worked as restaurant critic for France 3.


== Political career ==
Ramos has been a member of MoDem since 2008.In parliament, Ramos serves as a member of the Committee on Economic Affairs. In addition to his committee assignments, he is part of the French-Algerian Parliamentary Friendship Group and the French-Moroccan Parliamentary Friendship Group.
