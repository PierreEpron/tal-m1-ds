Rossella Panarese (23 October 1960 – 1 March 2021) was an Italian radio personality, programme editor, and speaker. In 2003, she conceived and managed the Radio3 Scienza program, for the Italian radio channel Rai Radio 3.She graduated from the International School for Advanced Studies, located in Trieste, Italy, and from La Sapienza University.She led the Science Festival at the Roma Tre University
