Garik Sephkhanyan  (Armenian: Գարիկ Սեփխանյան, born on August 13, 1983), is an Armenian actor and screenwriter. He is known for his role as barmen and cafe waiter Ruben on Full House (Armenian TV series), Garik on Yere1. He is also a screenwriter of Full House. He is married with Syune Hambardzumyan
