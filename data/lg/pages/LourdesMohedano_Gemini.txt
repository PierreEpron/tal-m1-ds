Lourdes Mohedano Sanchez de Mora (born 17 June 1995 in Córdoba) is a Spanish group rhythmic  gymnast.


== Career ==
Mohedano represents her nation at international competitions. She participated at the 2012 Summer Olympics. She also competed at world championships, including at the 2015  World Rhythmic Gymnastics Championships where she won the bronze medal in the all-around event. In 2016, she got a silver medal in the Rio Olympic Games, as the second best rhythm gymnastics group. Mohedano competed at the 2016 Summer Olympics in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil where she was member of the Spanish group (together with Elena López, Artemi Gavezou, Sandra Aguilar, Alejandra Quereda) that won silver medal in group-all around.
