Antonio Matera (born 11 October 1996) is an Italian footballer  who plays as a midfielder for Serie C Group C club U.S. Avellino 1912


== Club career ==
He made his professional debut in the Lega Pro for Barletta on 6 April 2014 in a game against Pontedera.On 11 July 2019 he signed a 2-year contract with Cavese.On 19 July 2021 he joined Avellino.
