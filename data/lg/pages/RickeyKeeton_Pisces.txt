Rickey Keeton (born March 18, 1957) is an American former professional right-handed pitcher. Keeton attended Western Hills High School in Cincinnati, Ohio, and Southern Illinois University in Carbondale, Ill. He was picked in the third round of the 1978 amateur draft and pitched parts of two seasons in the majors, 1980 and 1981, for the Milwaukee Brewers of the Major League Baseball (MLB).
