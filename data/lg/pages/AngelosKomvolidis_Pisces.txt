Angelos Komvolidis (Greek: Άγγελος Κομβολίδης; born 14 March 1988) is a Germany-born, Greek footballer who last played for Pontioi Katerini F.C.


== Career ==
Born in Stuttgart, Komvolidis previously played for Iraklis Thessaloniki F.C. in the Greek Super League.
