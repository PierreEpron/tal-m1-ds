Guram Gedekhauri (born 24 October 1963) is a Soviet wrestler. He competed in the men's Greco-Roman 100 kg at the 1988 Summer Olympics.Gedekhauri is the father of Zurabi Gedekhauri who is also a Greco-Roman wrestler.
