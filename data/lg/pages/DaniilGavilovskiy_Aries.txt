Daniil Yaroslavovich Gavilovskiy (Russian: Даниил Ярославович Гавиловский; born 21 March 1990) is a Russian former football goalkeeper.


== Club career ==
He made his Russian Football National League debut for FC Khimik Dzerzhinsk on 13 July 2013 in a game against FC Baltika Kaliningrad.
