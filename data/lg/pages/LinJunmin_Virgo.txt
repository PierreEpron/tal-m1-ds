Lin Junmin (Chinese: 林俊敏; pinyin: Lín Jùnmǐn; born 31 August 1996) is a Chinese sport shooter.He participated at the 2018 ISSF World Shooting Championships, winning the gold medal.He has qualified to represent China at the 2020 Summer Olympics.
