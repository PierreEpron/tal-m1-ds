Aleksandr Mikhaylovich Saprykin (Russian: Александр Михайлович Сапрыкин; 28 July 1946 – 4 May 2021) was a Russian volleyball player who competed for the Soviet Union in the 1972 Summer Olympics.In 1972 he was part of the Soviet team which won the bronze medal in the Olympic tournament. He played all seven matches.
