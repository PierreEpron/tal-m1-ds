Ayman Yahya Salem Ahmed, commonly known as Ayman Yahya (Arabic: أيمن يحيى سالم, born 14 May 2001) is a Saudi Arabian professional footballer who plays as a winger for Saudi Professional League club Al Nassr.


== Personal life ==
He was born from a Yemeni family in Riyadh. His older brother, Ali Yahya, is also a professional who plays for Al-Riyadh on loan from Al-Nassr.
