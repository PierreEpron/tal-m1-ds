Félix Antonio Sánchez (born August 3, 1981) is a former Major League Baseball pitcher who played in the major leagues briefly with the Chicago Cubs as a September callup in 2003.
With Double-A West Tenn in 2003, Sánchez had a 3.23 ERA in 30 games and was called up by the Cubs after rosters expanded in September. He appeared in 3 games and gave up 2 earned runs. On April 29, 2004, he was traded to the Detroit Tigers and spent the rest of the season with Double-A Erie. In 2005, he played for the High-A Lakeland Tigers in what would be his last professional season.
