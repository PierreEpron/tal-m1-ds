Hisaaki Nakamine (Japanese: 中峰 寿彰; born November 30, 1961 in Shibetsu, Hokkaido, Japan) is a Japanese curler, a two-time Pacific-Asian silver medallist (1992, 1996, 1997) and a three-time Japan men's champion (1997, 1998, 1999).
He played for Japan at the 1998 Winter Olympics, where the Japanese team finished in fifth place.
