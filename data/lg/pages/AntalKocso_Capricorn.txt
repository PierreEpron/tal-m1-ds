Antal Kocso (born 22 December 1962) is a former international speedway rider from Hungary.


== Speedway career ==
Kocso reached the final of the Speedway World Championship in the 1988 Individual Speedway World Championship.He rode in the top tier of British Speedway from 1989-1993, riding for Bradford Dukes. Bradford signed him following an impressive showing at the 1988 Speedway World Pairs Championship at Odsal, where he scored 15 points.He has been on the podium three times during the Hungarian National Championships.
