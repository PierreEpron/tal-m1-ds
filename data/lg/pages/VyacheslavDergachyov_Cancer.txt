Vyacheslav Anatolyevich Dergachyov (Belarusian: Вячаслаў Анатольевіч Дзергачоў; Russian: Вячеслав Анатольевич Дергачёв; born 1 July 2001) is a Belarusian professional footballer who plays for BATE Borisov.


== Honours ==
BATE Borisov

Belarusian Cup winner: 2020–21
Belarusian Super Cup winner: 2022
