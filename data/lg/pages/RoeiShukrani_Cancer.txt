Roei Shukrani (or Roee, Hebrew: רועי שוקרני; born 26 June 1990) is an Israeli footballer currently playing for Bnei Sakhnin.


== Early life ==
Shukrani was born in Karmi'el, Israel, to a family of Sephardic Jewish descent.
