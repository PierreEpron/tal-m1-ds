Blake Malone (born March 23, 2001) is an American soccer player who currently plays for Colorado Rapids 2 in MLS Next Pro.


== Career ==
Malone was part of the Seattle Sounders FC academy, and appeared for their USL affiliate side Seattle Sounders FC 2 during their 2018 season.After leaving the Seattle Sounders academy, Malone began to play college soccer at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 2019.In March 2021, Orange County SC loaned Malone to USL League One club Union Omaha for the 2021 season.
