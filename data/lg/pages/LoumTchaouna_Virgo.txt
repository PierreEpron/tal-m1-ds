Loum Tchaouna (born 8 September 2003) is a professional footballer who plays as a forward for  Ligue 2 club Dijon on loan from Rennes. Born in Chad, he represents France at international level.


== Club career ==
Tchaouna was born in N'Djamena, Chad. He played for youth teams of FC Kronenbourg, Schiltigheim, Strasbourg, and Rennes. He made his professional debut for Rennes on 26 September 2021 in a 1â€“1 Ligue 1 draw with Bordeaux.On 30 August 2022, Tchaouna joined Dijon on loan.


== International career ==
Born in Chad, Tchaouna moved to France at a young age. He is a youth international for France.


== Personal life ==
Loum's brother Haroun is also a professional footballer. He plays for the Chad national team.
