Bassam Kousa (Arabic: بسام كوسا; born 7 November 1954 in Aleppo) is a Syrian film and TV actor. He played many important roles in popular TV series including Bab al-Hara and Old Times. He is also an accomplished film actor.


== Personal life ==
Kousa is married to Thawra Alyoussef and has two children.
