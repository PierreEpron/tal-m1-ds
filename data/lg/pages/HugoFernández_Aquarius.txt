Hugo Daniel Fernández Vallejo (2 February 1945 – 1 August 2022) was an Uruguayan football player and manager.


== Career ==
Fernández played professional football in Uruguay, Argentina, Spain and Mexico. He played for Mexican side Puebla F.C. in 1979, before returning home with CA Peñarol.After he retired from playing, Fernández became a football coach. He has managed former club, Puebla, on three occasions. He has also managed Consadole Sapporo in Japan.
