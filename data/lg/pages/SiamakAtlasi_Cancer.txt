Ezzat Atlasifar (Persian: عزت اطلسی‌فر; 24 June 1936 – 26 September 2021), known professionally as Siamak Atlasi (Persian: سیامک اطلسی), was an Iranian actor and film director. 


== Biography ==
Atlasi started his artistic career in 1964 through the Association of Speakers and made his film debut in 1971 by acting in the movie Rough Men directed by Saber Rahbar.Despite being vaccinated, Atlasi died of COVID-19 on 26 September 2021.
