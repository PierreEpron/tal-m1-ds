Fábio Rocha Chiuffa (born 10 March 1989) is a Brazilian handball player who plays for HC Dobrogea Sud and the Brazilian handball team.He won two gold medals at the Pan American Games and competed at the 2016 Olympics.


== Individual awards and achievements ==
2016 Pan American Men's Handball Championship: Best right wing
2018 Pan American Men's Handball Championship: Best right wing, top scorer
2020 South and Central American Men's Handball Championship: Best right wing
