Muhamad Osamanmusa (Thai: มูฮัมหมัด อุสมานมูซา, born January 19, 1998) is a Thai futsal player. He plays for Córdoba CF Futsal in the Primera División de Futsal, the premier professional futsal league in Spain, and the Thailand national futsal team. He made his international debut at the 2016 AFF Futsal Championship.


== Early life ==
Muhammad was born to a Ghanaian father and a Thai mother in Bangkok. As his name implies, he is a Muslim. His father was also a footballer who played for the Thailand Tobacco Monopoly and the Port Authority of Thailand.
Muhammad lost his father when he was three years old. His maternal grandmother raised him while his mother worked abroad.
