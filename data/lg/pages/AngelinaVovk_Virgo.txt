Angelina Mikhaylovna Vovk  (Russian: Ангели́на Миха́йловна Вовк, IPA: [ɐnɡʲɪˈlʲinə mʲɪˈxajləvnə ˈvofk]; born September 16, 1942) is a former presenter for the Soviet Central Television active from 1980, best known for years on Good Night, Little Ones! and Pesnya goda. She was awarded People's Artist of the Russian Federation (2006).
