Hong Myong-hui  or Hong Myung-hee (born September 4, 1991,Korean: 홍명희) is a North Korean footballer who played as a goalkeeper for the North Korea women's national football team. She was part of the team at the 2011 FIFA Women's World Cup. At the club level, she played for 25 April in North Korea.
