Nikolay Vasiliyevich Vdovichenko (Russian: Николай Васильевич Вдовиченко; born 21 April 1989) is a Russian former professional football player forward. 


== Club career ==
In September 2020, he signed a contract with the Moldovan club FC Codru Lozova.
He made his Moldovan Super Liga debut for FC Codru Lozova on 20 September 2020 in a game against FC Sfîntul Gheorghe.In February 2021 he signed a contract with JK Narva Trans.In April 2022, he signed a one-year contract with FC Dynamo Vologda.
