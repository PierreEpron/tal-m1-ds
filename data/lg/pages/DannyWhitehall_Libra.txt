Danny Whitehall (born 8 October 1995) is an English professional footballer who plays for Eastleigh as a forward.


== Career ==
Born in Liverpool, Whitehall spent his early career with Liverpool and Rochdale. He joined Rochdale at under-18 level following his release from Liverpool.He began his senior career with  Southport, moving to Marine in September 2014. He moved on to Prescot Cables in November 2014.Whitehall then spent time in the United States, playing college soccer for SIU Edwardsville Cougars and Hastings College, and at club level for Chattanooga FC and Bugeaters FC. At Hastings he won a number of awards and set several records, scoring 93 goals in 75 games.He returned to England in July 2019 with Maidenhead United, moving to Scottish club Kilmarnock in July 2020 on a six-month contract. He made his debut as a substitute against Hibernian on 1 August 2020.
