Alvaro Salas (born May 25, 1953 in Montevideo, Uruguay) is a Uruguayan  Master Candombe drummer and percussion teacher.


== Early life ==
Salas was born in Ansina, a neighbourhood in  Palermo, Montevideo.


== Career in music industry ==
Salas has worked, both live and on records, with a number of musicians, including Eduardo Mateo,  Jorginho Gularte, Roberto Galletti, Federico Britos, and Urbano Moraes. He has also worked as a percussionist, arranger, and director with several award-winning Carnival groups including Marabunta, Sueno del Buceo, Vendaval, and others. He is the current Director of Afro-Uruguayan social organization Mundo Afro. As well, he runs the musical school in this institution, which has international prestige.
