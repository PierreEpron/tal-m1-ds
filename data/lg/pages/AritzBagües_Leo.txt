Aritz Bagües Kalparsoro (born 19 August 1989 in Errenteria) is a Spanish former cyclist, who competed as a professional from 2011 to 2022. He competed in the Vuelta a España each year from 2018 to 2021. He now works as a directeur sportif for UCI ProTeam Caja Rural–Seguros RGA.
