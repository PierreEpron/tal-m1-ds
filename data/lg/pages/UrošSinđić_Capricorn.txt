Uroš Sinđić (Serbian Cyrillic: Урош Синђић; born 19 January 1986) is a Serbian professional footballer who plays as a defensive midfielder.


== Career ==
In June 2017, Sinđić returned to Mladost Lučani after almost a decade. He helped the club reach the 2017–18 Serbian Cup final, eventually losing 2–1 to Partizan.


== Honours ==
Mladost LučaniSerbian First League: 2006–07
Serbian Cup: Runner-up 2017–18
