Andreas Ertl (born 30 September 1975) is a German alpine skiing coach and former alpine skier who won a gold medal with the national team at the team event at the 2005 Alpine World Ski Championships.He participates at six editions of the Alpine Ski World Championships from 1996 to 2005.


== Biography ==
Andreas Ertl is the brother of the German alpine ski champion Martina Ertl.
