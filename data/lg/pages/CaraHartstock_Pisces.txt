Cara Hartstock (born 18 March 1994) is a German handball player for Buxtehuder SV in the Frauen Handball-Bundesliga.
Hartstock represented the German junior national team, where she participated at the 2014 Women's Junior World Handball Championship, placing 4th.In February 2022, she signed a 1-year contract with the Bundesliga club Buxtehuder SV. She has previously played for HSG Blomberg-Lippe, TuS Metzingen, VfL Oldenburg and HC Leipzig.
