Daan Heymans (born 15 June 1999) is a Belgian professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for Charleroi.


== Club career ==
On 27 January 2022, he joined Charleroi on a 1.5-year loan with an option to buy.On 3 July 2022, Heymans moved to Charleroi on a permanent basis and signed a three-year contract.
