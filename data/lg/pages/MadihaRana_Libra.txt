Madiha Rana (Urdu: مدیحہ رانا; born 15 October 1986) is a Pakistani politician who was a Member of the Provincial Assembly of the Punjab, from May 2013 to May 2018.


== Early life and education ==
She was born on 15 October 1986 in Kuwait.She graduated in 2010 form the University of the Punjab and has the degree of Bachelor of Arts.


== Political career ==
She was elected to the Provincial Assembly of the Punjab as a candidate of Pakistan Muslim League (N) on a reserved seat for women in 2013 Pakistani general election.
