Mohammed Yahya Majrashi (Arabic: محمد يحيى مجرشي; born 20 May 1991) is a Saudi Arabian professional footballer who plays as a striker for Al-Fayha. He played at the 2011 FIFA U-20 World Cup and 2013 AFC U-22 Asian Cup qualification.
