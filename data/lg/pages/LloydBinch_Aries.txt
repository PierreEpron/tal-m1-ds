== Career ==
He competed in the sprint event at the 1960 Summer Olympics. He also represented England and won a bronze medal in the 1,000m match sprint at the 1958 British Empire and Commonwealth Games in Cardiff, Wales.Binch was a seven times British track champion, winning the British National Individual Sprint Championships from 1955 until 1961.
