Georgi Valeryevich Natabashvili (Russian: Георгий Валерьевич Натабашвили; born 21 February 2000) is a Russian football player of Georgian descent. He plays for FC Alania Vladikavkaz and FC Alania-2 Vladikavkaz.


== Club career ==
He made his debut in the Russian Football National League for FC Alania Vladikavkaz on 1 August 2020 in a game against FC SKA-Khabarovsk, as a starter.
