Aleksandr Nikolayevich Kashtanov (Russian: Алекса́ндр Никола́евич Кашта́нов; 25 March 1928 – 8 February 2022) was a Soviet and Russian agronomist and politician.
A member of the Communist Party, he served on the Supreme Soviet of the Russian SFSR from 1971 to 1980. He died on 8 February 2022, at the age of 93.
