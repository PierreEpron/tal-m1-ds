Hoàng Minh Tâm (born 28 October 1990) is a Vietnamese footballer who plays as a central midfielder for V.League 1 club SHB Đà Nẵng and Vietnam national football team.


== Honours ==
SHB Đà Nẵng

V.League 1: Runner-up 2013
Vietnamese National Cup: Runner-up 2013
