Robyn Jones Horner (born Robyn Leigh Jones; January 1, 1985) is an American soccer player. She is a goalkeeper. Jones previously played two years for the Philadelphia Independence of Women's Professional Soccer. The 2015 season marked Jones' fourth season with the Charlotte Lady Eagles.Horner grew up in the Titusville section of Hopewell Township, Mercer County, New Jersey and played high school soccer at The Pennington School.
