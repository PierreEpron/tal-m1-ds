Paulo César Villar Nieto (born 28 July 1978 in Santa Marta, Magdalena) is a Colombian hurdling athlete.At the 2006 Central American and Caribbean Games he finished second in 13.29 seconds, equalling the South American record of Brazilian Redelén dos Santos as well as establishing a new national record.


== Personal bests ==
100 m: 10.69 s (wind: +1.4 m/s) –  Medellín, 25 April 2009
200 m: 21.29 s (wind: NWI) –  Bogotá, 13 August 2006
110 m hurdles: 13.27 s (wind: +1.6 m/s) –  Guadalajara, 28 October 2011
400 m hurdles: 50.34 s –  Trujillo, 28 November 2013
