Sandro Bregadze (Georgian: სანდრო ბრეგაძე) is a Georgian politician. He was also a Member of Parliament from 2000 to 2004. From 2014 to 2016 he served as the deputy minister in the State Ministry on Diaspora Issues of Georgia. In 2017, he established the nationalist organisation Georgian March, which became a political party in 2020 and participated in legislative elections.
