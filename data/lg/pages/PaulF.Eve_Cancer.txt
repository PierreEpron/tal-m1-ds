== Biography ==
Paul F. Eve was born in Richmond County, Georgia on June 27, 1806.He graduated from Franklin College in 1826, and from the University of Pennsylvania Medical School in 1828. He participated in November Uprising.He published over 600 articles in books and medical journals, and was the editor of the Southern Medical and Surgical Journal.Eve died in Nashville on November 3, 1877.
