Matías Alejandro Mayer (born 11 March 1996) is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for Bella Vista .


== Career ==
Mayer spent time in the youth systems of Pacífico and San Martín, prior to joining Olimpo in 2012. In 2015, Mayer was loaned to Torneo Federal B side Bella Vista. He played one match for the club before returning to Olimpo. During the 2016–17 Argentine Primera División campaign, Mayer made his professional debut during a goalless draw with former club San Martín on 3 April 2017.In October 2020, Mayer joined Sansinena. In 2021, Mayer returned to his former club, Bella Vista.
