Gerhard Körner (born 20 September 1941) is a German former footballer.
He played 276 matches in the Oberliga and scored 50 goals for Vorwärts Berlin and FC Vorwärts Frankfurt/Oder respectively.Gerhard Körner won 33 caps for East Germany.
