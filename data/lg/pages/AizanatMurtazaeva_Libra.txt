Aizanat Makhachevna Murtazaeva (Russian: Айзанат Махачевна Муртазаева; born 23 September 2001 in Khunzakh, Dagestan) is a Russian right-handed épée fencer and 2021 Olympian.Murtazaeva competed in the 2020 Tokyo Olympic Games, where she placed fourth in individual women's épée. She won two bronze medals at the 2019 and 2021 Russian Championships.
