Mohamed Abdel-Galil (Arabic: محمد عبدالجليل) is a former Egyptian international midfielder. He spent his playing career with Zamalek SC and Al-Ahly.
Mohamed made some appearances for the Egypt national football team, including 1994 African Nations Cup matches.
