Antonio Ghomsi (born 22 April 1986) is a Cameroonian former footballer who played as a left-back.


== Biography ==
Ghomsi started his career at Genoa. In July 2006, he was signed by Messina of Serie A. He made his Serie A debut on 1 October against Livorno. In summer 2007 he left for Juve Stabia of Serie C1. He became a free agent after Messina bankrupted in July 2008. In October 2008, he was signed by Avellino of Serie B on free transfer.In July 2009, he was signed by A.C. Siena of Serie A. and pending a loan deal to K.V. Mechelen. He made his Belgian First Division debut on 1 August 2009, K.V. Mechelen 4–1 won K.V.C. Westerlo.
