Viktor Eyrikhovich Gaus (Russian: Виктор Эйрихович Гаус; born 25 November 1964) is a Russian professional football coach and a former player. He is the goalkeepers' coach with FC Krylia Sovetov Samara.


== Club career ==
He made his professional debut in the Soviet First League in 1987 for FC Krylia Sovetov Kuybyshev.He made his Russian Premier League debut for Krylia Sovetov on 29 March 1992 in a game against FC Spartak Moscow and played 4 seasons in the RPL with Krylia Sovetov and FC Lada Togliatti.
