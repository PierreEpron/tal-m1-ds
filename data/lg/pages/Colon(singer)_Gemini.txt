Colon (ころん, Koron, born May 29, 1996) is a Japanese singer and live streamer. He is a member of the J-pop group Strawberry Prince.


== Biography ==
Colon was recruited by Nanamori to join Strawberry Prince in mid-2016. On January 27, 2021, he released his first solo album, Aster (Japanese: アスター). The album sold 94,000 copies in its debut week and placed first in the Oricon weekly album ranking.Colon runs a gaming channel on YouTube. He was the most tweeted-about gaming personality in the world in the first half of 2021.
