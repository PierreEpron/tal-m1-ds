Zheng Tian Hui (Chinese: 郑天慧; pinyin: Zhèngtiānhuì; born 24 January 1986) is a retired professional wushu taolu athlete from Hong Kong. She is a ten-time medalist at the World Wushu Championships and a three-time world champion. She also won a silver medal at the 2010 Asian Games.
