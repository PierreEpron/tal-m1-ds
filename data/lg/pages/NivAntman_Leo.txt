Niv Antman (Hebrew: ניב אנטמן; born 2 August 1992) is an Israeli footballer currently playing in Sektzia Ness Ziona.


== Career ==
Antman, the son of veteran goalkeeper Giora Antman, played in his youth career in Maccabi Tzur Shalom and Maccabi Haifa, before moving to Hapoel Haifa. Antman graduated to the senior team in 2012 and got his break at the first team as replacement for an injured first goalkeeper Tvrtko Kale, playing 6 full matches during the 2013–14 season. In 2015, Antman returned to the team's starting lineup.In 2011, Antman played two matches with the Israel U19 team, against Belarus.
