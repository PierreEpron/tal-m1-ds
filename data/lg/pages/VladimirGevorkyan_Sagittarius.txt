Vladimir Gevorkyan (Belarusian: Уладзімір Геваркян, Russian: Владимир Геворкян, Armenian: Վլադիմիր Գեւորգյան; born 14 December 1955 in Brest) is a Belarusian professional football coach and a former player of Armenian descent. In 2015, he worked as a head coach of Dnepr Mogilev.


== Honours ==
Dinamo Brest (as coach)

Belarusian Cup winner: 2006–07
