== Biography ==
Van Raaij was born in Haps on 29 August 1936. He was a proponent of the Atlantic League, which would have been a competition for larger clubs in smaller European countries. Professionally, he was a manager at Philips.Van Raaij died on 16 November 2020 at the age of 84.
